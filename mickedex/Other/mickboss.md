# Mickboss

**Type:** Dark / Normal  
**Classification:** Boss Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m)  
**Weight:** 165.3 lbs (75.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Boss Mickémon. Wears sunglasses and sits in a tiny chair. Demands respect and snacks."

### Biology
Mickboss is a self-proclaimed "big shot" Mickus that wears sleek black sunglasses and sits atop a comically small throne or office chair. It has a thick cigar-like stick (actually a rolled-up leaf) in its mouth and exudes an aura of authority. Despite its small size, it acts like the boss of any group it joins, demanding tribute in the form of snacks and respect. It loves giving orders and making "deals" with other Mickémon, though most of its schemes fall apart due to its inherently derpy nature.

### Evolution

Evolves after defeating 3 wild Mickus in consecutive fights.
