# Micksweet

**The Sugar Rush Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Sugar Rush Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Sugar Rush Mickémon. Vibrates uncontrollably after eating too many Rare Candies. Moves at blinding speeds."

### Biology
Micksweet is a hyperactive Mickus suffering from an extreme sugar rush after consuming too many Rare Candies. Its eyes are wide and jittery, its body vibrates constantly, and it moves at blinding speeds, often crashing into things or running in frantic circles. It has trouble staying still for even a second and speaks (or rather, micks) at an accelerated pace. After the sugar high wears off, it usually collapses into a deep sleep.

### Evolution

Micksweet evolves from Mickus when 10 or more Rare Cnadies are used on it.
