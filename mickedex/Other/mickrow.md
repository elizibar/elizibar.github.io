# Mickrow

**The Bully Frog Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Dark  
**Classification:** Bully Frog Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 143.3 lbs (65.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Bully Frog Mickémon. Wears a tiny crown and headbutts everything."

### Biology
Mickrow is a crowned bully frog Mickus with a smug expression and a tiny golden crown perched on its head. It loves headbutting anything and everything in sight, claiming it is "asserting dominance." Despite its aggressive attitude, it is surprisingly easy to distract with shiny objects or snacks. Mickrow acts like it rules whatever pond or body of water it currently occupies, even if it’s just a small puddle.

### Evolution

Mickrow evolves from Mickus when a King's Rock is used on it.
