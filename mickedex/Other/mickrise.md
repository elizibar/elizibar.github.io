# Mickrise

**The Morning Mickémon**

**Type:** Psychic / Fire  
**Classification:** Morning Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'07" (1.1 m)  
**Weight:** 66.1 lbs (30.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Morning Mickémon. Full of hope and caffeine. Its dawn cries wake up the entire neighborhood."

### Biology
Mickrise is a bright and optimistic morning Mickus filled with hope and energy. Its body glows with a warm golden light, and small flames flicker gently around its head like a sunrise. It greets each dawn with loud, cheerful cries that wake up the entire neighborhood. Mickrise is full of motivation and tries to inspire everyone around it, though its overly enthusiastic wake-up calls are not always appreciated.

### Evolution

Mickrise evolves from Mickus if it levels up to 33 or higher at Dawn.
