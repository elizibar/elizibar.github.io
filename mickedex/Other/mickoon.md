# Mickoon

**The Lunar Howler Mickémon**

**Type:** Dark / Fairy  
**Classification:** Lunar Howler Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m)  
**Weight:** 77.2 lbs (35.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Lunar Howler Mickémon. Howls beautifully at night and sparkles under moonlight."

### Biology
Mickoon is a mysterious nocturnal Mickus with elegant crescent moon antennae and soft, glowing silver fur. It only appears on clear nights and leaves trails of sparkling lunar dust as it moves. Mickoon is known for its beautiful, haunting howls that echo across the landscape. While it tries to maintain an air of mystery, it still retains the classic Mickus clumsiness, often tripping over its own antennae while trying to look graceful under the moonlight.

### Evolution

Mickoon evolves from Mickus when a Moon Stone is used on it.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Moon Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Lunar Mick
