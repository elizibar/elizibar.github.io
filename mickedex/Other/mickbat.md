# Mickbat

**The Cave Bat Mickémon**

**Type:** Flying / Rock  
**Classification:** Cave Bat Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m)  
**Weight:** 99.2 lbs (45.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Cave Bat Mickémon. Has mullet-shaped ears. Loves dramatic entrances from the ceiling."

### Biology
Mickbat is a cave-dwelling Mickus with large, mullet-shaped ears that function as highly sensitive sonar. Its body is covered in dark rocky fur, allowing it to blend into cave walls. It enjoys hanging upside down from ceilings and dropping dramatically onto unsuspecting Trainers or Mickémon below. Mickbat is theatrical and loves making grand, spooky entrances, often accompanied by a loud "MIIIIIIICK!" echo through the cave.
