# Partymick

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Party Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 12.1 lbs (5.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Party Mickémon. Always has a party whistle in its mouth. Ready to celebrate anything."

### Biology
Partymick is a regular Mickus that has a party whistle permanently stuck in its mouth. It is always ready to celebrate, regardless of the situation, whether winning a battle, losing one, or simply waking up in the morning. Its eyes are wide with excitement, and it frequently blows its whistle enthusiastically, creating loud, shrill sounds that can energize allies or annoy opponents.

### Evolution

Partymick evolves from Mickus if it is traded for another Mickus.  If Partymick is traded for another Partymick, they both evolve into Ravemick.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Traded for another Mickus)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Ravemick (mutual trade with another Partymick)
