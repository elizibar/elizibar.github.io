# Ultra Mick

**The Chaos God Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal (changes every turn)  
**Classification:** Chaos God Mickémon  
**Height:** 6'07" (2.0 m)  
**Weight:** 264.6 lbs (120.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Chaos God Mickémon. The final form of Mickus. Its body contains pieces of every evolution."

### Biology
Ultra Mick is the ultimate and most chaotic form of the Mickus line. Its body is a swirling, ever-shifting rainbow mass that incorporates visual traits from almost every known Mickus evolution, with pieces of leather jackets, water twin-tails, flaming guitar fragments, tiny wrestling boots, robotic plating, glowing heart symbols, and more. A magnificent rainbow mullet flows from its head, and its eyes constantly shift colors. Ultra Mick is pure concentrated chaos.  It changes type every few seconds and has been known to scream random internet memes, song lyrics, and battle cries all at once during fights. It is considered the physical embodiment of everything a Mickus can become.

### Evolution

Ultra Mick's evolution is complex and requires a Mickus to be teamed with 5 other evolved Mickus types, and then visiting several of the biomes in the Cordia region.  The final step is believed to be a visit to Mount Lucas.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus / Minimick / Mickey / Bigmick
