# Ravemick

**The Rave Mickémon**

**Type:** Psychic  
**Classification:** Rave Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Rave Mickémon. Holds glowsticks and has swirling hypnotic eyes. Can party for days without stopping."

### Biology
Ravemick is a wild, energetic Mickus with hypnotic swirling patterns in its eyes and glowsticks held in both hands. Its body pulses with psychedelic colors and it can party for days without stopping. It projects colorful light patterns during battle and moves with erratic, high-energy dancing motions. Ravemick is always in party mode and tries to drag other Mickémon into spontaneous raves, regardless of the situation.

### Evolution

Ravemick evolves when two Partymicks are traded for each other.  Both will become Ravemicks.
