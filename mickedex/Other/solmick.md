# Solmick

**The Solar Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire / Psychic  
**Classification:** Solar Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 110.2 lbs (50.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Solar Mickémon. The ultimate daytime form. So bright it needs sunglasses just to look at itself."

### Biology
Solmick is the pinnacle of the sunny evolution line. Its body constantly emits intense, golden light, making it one of the brightest Mickémon in existence. It has a majestic, flowing mane of solar flames and wears what appears to be living sunglasses formed from concentrated sunlight. Solmick is incredibly energetic during the day, practically glowing with power, but becomes noticeably drowsy and dimmer as night falls. Many Trainers say simply being near a Solmick feels like standing in the warmth of a perfect summer day.

### Evolution

Solmick evolves from Micksol when Micksol levels during the Daytime.  It has no further evolutions.
