# Mickrystal

**Type:** Psychic / Rock  
**Classification:** Crystal Skull Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Crystal Skull Mickémon. It believes it comes from the stars and constantly tries to communicate with aliens that may or may not exist."

### Biology
Mickrystal is a translucent, glowing crystal skull with a Mickus face inside. Its body is made of shimmering crystal with faint psychic energy swirling within. It floats slightly off the ground and has tiny crystal hands. It is convinced it is an alien being sent to Earth long ago and constantly tries to communicate with "the mothership" using psychic signals.

It has powerful Psychic abilities but is still very much a Mickus. It gets distracted easily, forgets what it was doing, and sometimes just floats in circles humming alien-sounding tunes. It loves shiny objects and believes they are "communication devices."

### Evolution
Is born when a Crystal Skull is Revived in a Laboratory.
