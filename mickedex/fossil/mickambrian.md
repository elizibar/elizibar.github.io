### Short Mickédex Entry

### Biology
Mickambrian is a bizarre, patchwork Fossil Mickus based on the mysterious prehistoric creature Hallucigenia. It has a long, spiky body with legs pointing in multiple directions, a single large eye on a stalk, and its signature hat somehow fused to one of its spikes. It is constantly confused about its own body plan. It frequently tries to walk in the wrong direction, gets its spikes tangled, and loudly questions its own existence: "Why am I like this?!"

Despite its awkward construction, it has surprisingly strong Psychic powers and can levitate or move objects with its mind while trying to sort out its own limbs. It is friendly but perpetually bewildered, often staring at its own body in disbelief.
