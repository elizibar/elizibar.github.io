# Mickburger

**Type:** Fire / Normal  
**Classification:** Burger Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 33.1 lbs (15.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Burger Mickémon. Trained by the Duke of the Grill. It is a living hamburger that works as a fry cook, flipping burgers with expert skill."

### Biology
Mickburger is a living hamburger with a sesame seed bun, lettuce, cheese, and a perfectly cooked patty. It has tiny arms and legs, a chef’s hat, and its signature mullet tuft poking out from under the top bun. It works as a fry cook at the Duke of the Grill and is incredibly skilled at flipping burgers, often doing fancy tricks with its spatula. It is cheerful and hardworking, but it gets very upset if anyone tries to eat it. Its body is always warm and smells delicious, which sometimes causes problems when wild Mickémon try to take a bite.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Duke of the Grill.
