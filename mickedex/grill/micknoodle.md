# Micknoodle

**The Ramen Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Psychic  
**Classification:** Ramen Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m) 
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
Micknoodle is a master chef that specializes in slurpy ramen but can also prepare sushi, sashimi, katsu, and other Kanto dishes with expert skill."


### Biology
Micknoodle is a living bowl of ramen with a cheerful Mickus face floating in the broth. Long, wavy noodles form its "hair" and mullet, and it wears a traditional chef’s headband. Its broth is always perfectly seasoned and steaming hot. It is a master chef that can instantly prepare delicious ramen, sushi, sashimi, katsu, and other Kanto dishes using its Psychic powers to manipulate ingredients at high speed. It loves feeding other Pokémon and Trainers and gets genuinely happy when people enjoy its food. In battle, it uses its broth and noodles as weapons, creating swirling ramen tornadoes or slapping opponents with psychic chopsticks.

### Evolution
Evolves when trained by the Duke of the Grill.
