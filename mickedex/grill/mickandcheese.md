# MIckandcheese

**The Comfort Food Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Fire  
**Classification:** Comfort Food Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"It is most famous for its gooey macaroni and cheese, but grilled cheese sandwiches are also on the menu."

### Biology
Mickandcheese is a warm, gooey, cheese-covered Mickus. Its body is a mix of creamy macaroni and melted cheese with a golden-brown baked crust on top. It has tiny arms and legs, a chef’s hat, and its signature mullet made of crispy cheese pull. It radiates warmth and comfort, and its presence can calm down agitated Pokémon. It is incredibly skilled at making macaroni and cheese and grilled cheese sandwiches, often handing them out to friends and allies after battles. It is gentle and nurturing but can get surprisingly spicy when provoked, heating up its cheese to burning temperatures.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Duke of the Grill.
