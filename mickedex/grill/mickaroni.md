# Mickaroni

**Type:** Normal / Fighting  
**Classification:** Pasta Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 121.3 lbs (55.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Pasta Master Mickémon. It serves up a mix of Unova-style alfredo and marinara dishes along with Alola favorites like macaroni salad. It is street tough but quick to call everyone 'brudda' and try to calm things down."

### Biology
Mickaroni is a tough, street-smart Mickus made of perfectly cooked pasta. Its body is a mix of alfredo-coated fettuccine and marinara-covered spaghetti with bits of macaroni salad mixed in. It wears a backwards cap and a stained apron, and its mullet is made of long, wavy pasta strands. It talks with its hands (literally gesturing wildly with pasta arms) and has a thick, mixed Unova-Alola accent. One minute it’s ready to throw down, the next it’s trying to de-escalate the situation while offering everyone a plate of pasta and calling them "brudda." It is fiercely loyal to its friends and will fight hard to protect them, but it would rather settle things over a good meal.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Duke of the Grill.
