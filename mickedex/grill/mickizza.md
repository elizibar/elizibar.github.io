# Mickizza

**The Pizza Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire / Grass  
**Classification:** Pizza Mickémon  
**Height:**  2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Pizza Mickémon. It is a living pizza that delivers hot, fresh slices with speedy enthusiasm."

### Biology
Mickpizza is a living, round pizza with a golden-brown crust, gooey melted cheese, and various toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.). It has tiny arms and legs, a delivery hat, and its signature mullet tuft made of basil leaves. It is incredibly fast on its tiny wheels (made from sliced sausage) and loves delivering hot, fresh pizza to Trainers and Mickémon alike. Its body is always warm and cheesy, and it can fire blasts of molten cheese or spinning pepperoni discs in battle. Like Mickburger, it gets very upset if anyone tries to eat it.

### Evolution
Evolves when trained by the Duke of the Grill.
