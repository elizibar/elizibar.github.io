# Mickma

**Type:** Fire / Ground  
**Classification:** Magma Kaiju Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #049  
**Height:** 6'07" (2.0 m)  
**Weight:** 485.0 lbs (220.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Magma Kaiju Mickémon. A walking volcano with a hat."

### Biology
Mickma is a massive, magma-covered kaiju version of Mickus. Its body constantly churns with molten rock and lava, leaving glowing footprints and small eruptions wherever it walks. Despite its destructive appearance and immense size, it still wears a slightly singed hat perched on its head. Mickma is surprisingly cheerful for a walking natural disaster and enjoys dramatic entrances.

### Evolution

Mickma evolves from Mickus in a Volcanic area.

Mickma evolves into Mickmagma at level 38, or into Mickblaze when a Magmarizer is used on it.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Leveled up in a Volcanic Area)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickmagma, Mickblaze
