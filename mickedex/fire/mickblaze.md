# Mickblaze

**Type:** Fire / Fighting  
**Classification:** Magma Wrestler Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'11" (1.8 m)  
**Weight:** 264.6 lbs (120.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Magma Wrestler Mickémon. Punches with flaming fists. Loves dramatic entrances."

### Biology
Mickblaze is a fiery, magma-covered wrestler whose body constantly radiates intense heat. Its fists are made of molten rock, and flames constantly lick across its muscular frame. It has a permanent cocky grin and loves making dramatic entrances by erupting from the ground or dropping from above with a flaming elbow drop. Even though its body temperature is dangerously high, it still insists on giving overly enthusiastic hugs to allies and opponents alike.
