# Mickizard Y

**Type:** Fire / Flying  
**Classification:** Double-Neck Guitarist Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #047  
**Height:** 6'03" (1.9 m)  
**Weight:** 176.4 lbs (80.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Double-Neck Mickémon. Even edgier with a massive double-necked guitar. Its solos last way too long."

### Biology
Mickizard Y is an even more extreme evolution, now wielding a massive double-necked flaming guitar. Its flames burn hotter and brighter, and its ego has grown proportionally with its power. It performs solos so long and self-indulgent that opponents have been known to fall asleep or simply give up out of sheer exhaustion. Mickizard Y believes every battle is a rock concert and it is the headliner.

### Evolution

Mickizard Y evolves from Mickizard at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickizard (Level 38)
