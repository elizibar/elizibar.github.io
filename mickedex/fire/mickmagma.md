# Mickmagma

**Type:** Fire / Rock  
**Classification:** Lava Beast Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 7'03" (2.2 m)  
**Weight:** 771.6 lbs (350.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Lava Beast Mickémon. Even bigger and hotter than Mickma."

### Biology
Mickmagma is a larger and far more unstable evolution of Mickma. Its body is a roiling mass of lava and hardened rock, constantly shifting and erupting. It radiates intense heat that can melt nearby objects and causes the ground to crack under its weight. Mickmagma is one of the most destructive members of the Mickus family, yet it still somehow maintains a derpy, happy expression beneath all the lava.

### Evolution

Mickmagma evolves from Mickma at level 38.
