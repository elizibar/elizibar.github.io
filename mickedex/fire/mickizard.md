# Mickizard

**The Edgy Guitarist Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire / Flying  
**Classification:** Edgy Guitarist Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 132.3 lbs (60.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Edgy Guitarist Mickémon. Plays a flaming guitar. Thinks it’s the protagonist of reality."

### Biology
Mickizard is a fiery, dragon-like Mickémon with a permanent scowl and a flaming guitar strapped to its back. Its body is covered in red and orange scales that glow with inner heat, and smoke constantly rises from its nostrils. Mickizard is convinced it is the main character of every battle and story. It performs overly dramatic guitar solos even when no one is watching, leaving scorch marks and melted rocks in its wake.

### Evolution

Mickizard evolves from Mickus upon use of a Fire Stone.  Mickizard evolves into Mickizard Y at level 38 and Mickdrake with the use of a Magmarizer.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Fire Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickizard Y (Level 38), Mickdrake (Magmamirizer)
