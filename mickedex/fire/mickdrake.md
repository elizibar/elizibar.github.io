# Mickdrake

**Type:** Dragon / Fire  
**Classification:** Legendary Dragon Mickémon  
**Height:** 7'07" (2.3 m)  
**Weight:** 485.0 lbs (220.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Legendary Dragon Mickémon. A majestic rockstar dragon whose flaming guitar solos can summon meteors."

### Biology
Mickdrake is a majestic, fully evolved dragon Mickus with massive flaming wings and a legendary flaming guitar fused to its body. Its scales shimmer with fiery energy, and it carries itself with the utmost confidence, convinced it is the main character of every battle and story. Its guitar solos are so powerful they can summon meteors or set the sky ablaze. Mickdrake’s ego is as large as its wingspan.

### Evolution

Mickdrake evolves from Mickizard when a Dragon Scale is used on it.
