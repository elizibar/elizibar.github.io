# Micknificent

**The Spectacular Star Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire/Fairy  
**Classification:** Spectacular Star Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 99.2 lbs (45.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Spectacular Star Mickémon. Only one Micknificent can exist in the world at a time. It is a flashy, over-the-top performer whose moves are so brilliant they can dazzle both allies and opponents."

### Biology
Micknificent is the ultimate show-off of the Mickus family. Its body is covered in dazzling, ever-shifting fiery sparkles and fairy lights. It wears an extravagant, glittering outfit that changes appearance depending on its mood, complete with a massive, flowing mullet that burns with colorful flames. Every movement it makes is theatrical and spectacular - it never simply walks when it can strut, twirl, or burst into a spontaneous dance number.

Micknificent lives for the spotlight and constantly tries to one-up itself with increasingly ridiculous and beautiful performances. It is incredibly charismatic but also incredibly extra, even simple actions like eating a berry become a five-minute choreographed routine.

### Evolution

Any of (Mic, Mic Drop, Micjagger, Miku, Miku-02, Dark Miku, Mickstar) level up while holding a Fairy Feather it starts a fight against the current Micknificent.  The winner of that battle becomes (or remains) Micknificent while the loser evolves back into a Mickus.

### Special Rule
There can only be one Micknificent in the world at any time.

If another eligible Mickus tries to evolve into Micknificent while one already exists, the current Micknificent is immediately summoned for an emergency Song and Dance Battle. The winner remains (or becomes) Micknificent. The loser reverts back into a humble Mickus and loses all progress toward this evolution until the next opportunity arises.

This makes Micknificent function as a regional Legendary in terms of exclusivity and drama.
