# Lady Miku

**The Princess Idol Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Fairy  
**Classification:** Princess Idol Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Princess Idol Mickémon. An elegant variant that only appears when a female Mickus uses a Dawn Stone."

### Biology
Lady Miku is an elegant and regal evolution of Miku. It wears flowing royal attire with water-based accents and performs graceful, refined concerts rather than high-energy pop shows. While it maintains the idol spirit, Lady Miku carries itself with noticeable poise and a slightly haughty demeanor. It is deeply caring toward Sir Mick and often performs duets with him, creating beautiful water-and-steel spectacles.

### Evolution
Lady Miku evolves from a Female Mickus that has a Dawn Stone used on it.  Evolves into Mickmarried when teamed with Sir Mick and it levels up, which consumes both of them and replaces them with a single Mickmarried.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Dawn Stone, Female)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickmarried (with Sir Mick)
