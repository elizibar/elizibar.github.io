# Dark Miku

**Type:** Water / Ghost  
**Classification:** Underground Rave Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 85.1 lbs (38.6 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Underground Rave Mickémon. Performs illegal late-night shows. Its beats are said to alter the brain chemistry of anyone who hears them."

### Biology
Dark Miku is a mysterious, black-and-neon variant of Miku that only emerges at night. Its twin-tails glow with eerie neon lights, and its holographic skirt shifts between deep purples and electric blues. It performs intense underground raves in hidden locations, with beats that can disorient opponents and entrance listeners. While it maintains the idol spirit, its performances have a haunting, almost hypnotic quality that leaves audiences questioning their life choices.

### Evolution

Dark Miku evolves from Miku when it levels up at Night.

**Previous Evolution:** Miku (Leveled up at night)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Micknificent (Fairy Feather)
