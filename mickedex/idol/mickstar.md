# Mickstar

**The Pop Superstar Mickémon**

**Type:** Sound / Normal  
**Classification:** Pop Superstar Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'05" (1.35 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Pop Superstar Mickémon. It interrupts battles to drop new singles. Its ego is larger than its fanbase."

### Biology
Mickstar is a flashy, spotlight-obsessed pop idol Mickus. Its body sparkles with stage lights, and it carries a large, glittering microphone wherever it goes. It has a massive ego and constantly interrupts battles, training sessions, and even quiet moments to drop new "singles" or strike dramatic poses. Mickstar lives for attention and applause, becoming noticeably weaker and sulky if ignored for too long.

### Evolution

Mickstar evolves from Miku when it levels while knowing Hyper Voice.
