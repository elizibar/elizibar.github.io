# Mic Drop

**Type:** Sound / Fairy  
**Classification:** Professional Diva Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 85.3 lbs (38.7 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Professional Diva Mickémon. A true superstar that has mastered the art of leaving the stage right after the best part."

### Biology
Mic Drop is the ultimate polished performer of the idol line. Its glitter is more refined, its movements are perfectly choreographed, and its microphone tail has gained even greater power and range. It possesses an impeccable sense of timing and knows exactly when to make a dramatic exit. Mic Drop’s signature move is dropping its tail mic at the perfect moment, creating an explosive combination of sound and flair that leaves audiences (and opponents) stunned.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mic at level 34.

**Previous Evolution:** Mic (Level 34)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Micknificent (Fairy Feather)
