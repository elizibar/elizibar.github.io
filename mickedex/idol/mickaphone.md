# Mickaphone

**The Smartphone Mickémon**

**Type:** Electric / Psychic  
**Classification:** Smartphone Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'07" (1.1 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Smartphone Mickémon. It is always checking its "socials" and taking selfies mid-battle."

### Biology
Mickaphone is a tall, sleek robotic Mickus whose body is dominated by a giant smartphone screen for a head. It is constantly tapping on its own screen, taking selfies, and checking imaginary messages. It uses psychic waves to "hack" into other electronic devices and can project holographic apps in battle. Despite its advanced technology, it still suffers from occasional dropped connections and low battery anxiety.

### Evolution

Mickaphone evolves from Mickro or Mickbot when it levels in the same party as a Mickintosh.
