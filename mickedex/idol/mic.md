# Mic

**Type:** Sound / Fairy  
**Classification:** Disco Idol Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'01" (1.25 m)  
**Weight:** 79.4 lbs (36.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Disco Idol Mickémon. Its tail is a functioning microphone. Legendary for dramatic mic drops that cause actual explosions."

### Biology
Mic is a flashy, glitter-covered Mickus whose tail has transformed into a giant, fully functional microphone. Its body sparkles constantly with stage lights and sequins, and it moves with exaggerated theatrical flair. Mic lives for dramatic performances and is known for dropping its microphone tail at the climax of battles, which is an action that creates powerful sound waves capable of knocking out opponents. It is always seeking the spotlight.

### Evolution

Mic evolves from Mickus when a Shiny Stone is used on it while it has a High Beauty stat.  It evolves into Mic Drop at level 34.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Shiny Stone + high Beauty)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mic Drop (Level 34), Micknificent (Fairy Feather)
