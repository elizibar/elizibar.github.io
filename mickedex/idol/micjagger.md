# Micjagger

**Type:** Rock  
**Classification:** Swagger Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 114.6 lbs (52.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Swagger Mickémon. It struts with legendary swagger and performs moves so smooth they can make opponents trip over their own feet."

### Biology
Micjagger is a flashy, high-energy Rock-type Mickus with an unmistakable swagger. It has a wild, flowing mullet, wears a sparkly open-chested vest, and moves with the smoothest, most exaggerated dance steps imaginable. Its hips sway constantly, even when standing still. Micjagger is famous for its incredible stage presence and ability to make any movement look effortlessly cool. It loves challenging opponents to dance battles and gets visibly annoyed when people don’t appreciate its legendary moves.

Despite its cocky attitude, Micjagger is a dedicated performer who practices its dance moves for hours every day. It has been known to cause small rockslides simply by doing overly dramatic pelvic thrusts.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mic when Mic knows a Dance-type move and levels.  Sword Dance, Rain Dance, Feather Dance, etc.
