# Miku-02

**Type:** Water / Fairy  
**Classification:** Concert Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #016  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 92.6 lbs (42.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Upgraded Idol Mickémon. Now features better lighting, auto-tune, and dramatic wind effects. Its concerts sell out in seconds."

### Biology
Miku-02 is a greatly upgraded version of Miku, featuring enhanced holographic projectors, brighter stage lighting, and more advanced auto-tune capabilities. Its twin-tails flow more dramatically, and it can create dazzling water-and-light spectacles during battle. Miku-02 is considered the pinnacle of idol performance in the Mickus family and carries itself with noticeable star power and confidence.

### Evolution

Miku-02 evolves from Miku at level 32 if it has High Beauty.

**Previous Evolution:** Miku (Level 32 with high Beauty)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Micknificent (Fairy Feather)
