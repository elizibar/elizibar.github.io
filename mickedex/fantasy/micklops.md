# Micklops

**The Cyclops Mickémon**

**Type:** Ground / Dark  
**Classification:** Cyclops Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'11" (1.5 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Cyclops Mickémon. It is a one-eyed giant that loves staring contests and smashing things with its club."

### Biology
Micklops is a large, one-eyed cyclops Mickus with rocky gray skin, a single big eye in the center of its forehead, and a massive club made from a tree trunk. Its mullet tuft is wild and unkempt, and it still wears its signature hat (now comically small on its large head). It loves staring contests and smashing rocks (or sometimes other Mickémon) with its club. Despite its intimidating size and strength, it is surprisingly friendly and gets lonely easily, often trying to make friends by offering "gifts" of smashed rocks.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickoblin when it levels up while holding Soft Sand.
