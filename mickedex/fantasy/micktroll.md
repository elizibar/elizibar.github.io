# Micktroll

**Type:**  Rock / Dark  
**Classification:** Bridge Troll Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 176.4 lbs (80.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Bridge Troll Mickémon. It loves guarding bridges and demanding tolls from anyone who wants to cross."

### Biology
Micktroll is a large, brutish troll Mickus with rocky gray skin, a big nose, and a messy mullet. It carries a massive wooden club and wears a tattered loincloth with its signature hat perched on its head. It has a habit of standing on bridges and demanding tolls (usually snacks or shiny objects) from anyone who wants to pass. It is grumpy and loud but surprisingly easy to bribe. Despite its intimidating size and club, it still has the classic Mickus clumsiness and often trips over its own feet or accidentally smashes the bridge it’s supposed to be guarding.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickoblin when it levels while holding a Hard Stone.
