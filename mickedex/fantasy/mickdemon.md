# Mickdemon

**The Demonic Mickémon**

**Type:** Dark / Fire  
**Classification:** Demon Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Demon Mickémon. It is a mischievous demon that loves making deals and causing chaos with its fiery powers."

### Biology
Mickdemon is a small, impish demon Mickus with red skin, tiny horns, bat-like wings, and a flaming mullet. It still wears its signature hat, now slightly singed. It loves making mischievous deals with other Mickémon and Trainers, often offering "power" in exchange for snacks or shiny objects. Its flames are hot but not lethal, and are mostly used for dramatic effect and minor pranks. Despite its demonic appearance, it retains the classic Mickus derpiness and often gets comically overexcited, accidentally setting its own wings on fire.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickobln at level 40.
