# Mickgriffin

**The Noble Griffin Mickémon**

**Type:** Flying / Ground  
**Classification:** Noble Griffin Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 143.3 lbs (65.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"It is a proud and noble griffin that protects the weak with its sharp talons and powerful wings."

### Biology
Mickgriffin is a majestic, noble griffin Mickus with the body of a lion and the wings and head of an eagle. It has a flowing mullet mane, sharp talons, and still wears its signature hat (now perched regally between its ears). It carries itself with great dignity and is fiercely protective of smaller or weaker Pokémon. Despite its noble appearance and heroic attitude, it still has the classic Mickus clumsiness and often gets its wings tangled or dramatically overshoots landings.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickoblin when levelling while holding a Sharp Beak.
