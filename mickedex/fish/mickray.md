# Mickray

**Type:** Water/Electric  
**Classification:** Stingray Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 77.2 lbs (35.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Stingray Mickémon. It glides gracefully through the water while delivering powerful electric shocks with its tail."

### Biology
Mickray is a large, graceful stingray-like Mickus with wide, wing-like fins and a long, electric-charged tail. Its mullet has become a dramatic crest along its back, and it still wears its hat (now perched elegantly between its eyes). It glides through the water with smooth, majestic movements and can deliver powerful electric shocks through its tail. Despite its elegant appearance, it retains the classic Mickus clumsiness and sometimes overshoots turns and crashes into coral reefs or boats.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickrel at level 33.
