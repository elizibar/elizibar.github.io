# Mickeel

**Type:** Water  
**Classification:** Eel Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #106  
**Height:** 2'07" (0.8 m)  
**Weight:** 15.4 lbs (7.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Eel Mickémon. It is a long, slippery eel-like Mickus that loves zipping through water at high speeds."

### Biology
Mickeel is a long, sleek, eel-like Mickus with a slippery body covered in glowing blue patterns. Its mullet tuft has become a fin-like crest running down its back, and it still wears its signature hat (now streamlined for swimming). It is incredibly fast and agile in water, often zipping around like a living torpedo while making high-pitched "mick mick mick!" sounds. Despite its sleek appearance, it retains the classic Mickus derpiness and frequently gets stuck in tight spaces or dramatically flops when trying to show off on land.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus if it levels while holding Mystic Water.  Evolves into Mickrel if it evolves while holding a Deep Sea Scale, or Micktopus with a Deep Sea Tooth.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Mystic Water)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickrel (Deep Sea Scale), Micktopus (Deep Sea Tooth)
