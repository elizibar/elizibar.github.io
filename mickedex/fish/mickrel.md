# Mickrel

**The Mackerel Mickémon**

**Type:** Water  
**Classification:** Mackerel Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 33.1 lbs (15.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Mackerel Mickémon. It is a fast, silvery fish that loves swimming in large schools."

### Biology
Mickrel is a sleek, silvery mackerel-like Mickus with a streamlined body and a prominent mullet fin running down its back. It still wears its signature hat, now modified into a hydrodynamic shape. It is incredibly fast and loves swimming in large, chaotic schools, often leading them in dramatic synchronized patterns. It has a cheerful personality and is known for its impressive leaping ability and it can jump out of the water to "high-five" Trainers on boats.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickeel when it evolves while holding a Deep Sea Scale.  Evolves into Mickray at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickeel (Deep Sea Scale)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickray (Lv 38)
