# Mickraken

**The Kraken Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Dark  
**Classification:** Kraken Mickémon  
**Height:** 8'02" (2.5 m)  
**Weight:** 485.0 lbs (220.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Kraken Mickémon. It is a massive, legendary sea monster that loves dramatic entrances and wrapping boats with its tentacles."

### Biology
Mickraken is a massive, terrifying-yet-derpy kraken Mickus with enormous tentacles and glowing eyes. Its mullet has become a dramatic crest of dark seaweed, and its hat has grown into a massive, barnacle-covered crown. It loves making dramatic entrances by rising from the depths and wrapping its tentacles around anything nearby (ships, trainers, other Mickémon). Despite its fearsome reputation, it is still fundamentally a Mickus and gets comically upset when its dramatic moments are ruined by seagulls or bad timing.

Evolves from Micktopus at level 38.
