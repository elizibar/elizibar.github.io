# Micktopus

**The Octopus Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Dark  
**Classification:** Octopus Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Octopus Mickémon. It is a mischievous octopus that loves tangling things with its tentacles."



### Biology
Micktopus is a clever, eight-tentacled Mickus with a prominent mullet crest and its signature hat perched on its mantle. Its tentacles are highly dexterous and it loves using them to tangle up opponents, steal items, or create dramatic poses. It has a playful but mischievous personality and enjoys pranking other Mickémon by ink-clouding the water and then stealing their lunch. Despite its cunning nature, it still has the classic Mickus clumsiness and its tentacles often get tangled with each other.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickeel when it levels while holding a Deep Sea Fang.  Evolves into Mickraken at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickeel (Deep Sea Fang)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickraken (Level 38)
