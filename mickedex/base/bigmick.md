# Bigmick

**The Largest Gremlin Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Largest Gremlin Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'10" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Largest Gremlin Mickémon. Even bigger than Mickey. Its only purpose seems to be wasting Rare Candies and Trainer patience."

### Biology
Bigmick is the largest of the base Mickus forms. It looks almost exactly like Mickus and Mickey, only scaled up further. Its mullet is especially voluminous, and its hat appears slightly comically small on its bigger head. Despite its imposing size, Bigmick is still just as clumsy, derpy, and chaotic as its smaller relatives. It has a habit of accidentally knocking things over simply by existing in the same room. Trainers who manage to evolve a Mickus all the way to Bigmick often receive sympathetic looks from other trainers.

### Evolution

Bigmick evolves from Mickey at level 60.  Upon evolving into Bigmick, it is returned to level 1.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickey (Level 60)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Almost any other Mickus evolution.
