# Mickus

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Derpy Gremlin Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #001  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 12.1 lbs (5.5 kg)

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Derpy Gremlin Mickémon. A round, potato-shaped creature with a mullet tuft and a permanent hat. It copies its Trainer’s every action, usually at the worst possible moment."

### Biology
Mickus is a small, round, potato-like creature with stubby limbs, enormous sparkling eyes, and a single rebellious mullet tuft poking out from beneath a hat that seems permanently fused to its head. It is the ultimate chaotic blank slate, endlessly curious and possessing a strong instinct to mimic whatever its Trainer does. This copying behavior frequently leads to slapstick chaos, such as trying to battle when its Trainer is eating or attempting to cook when its Trainer is training. The hat is believed to be an integral part of its body, possibly storing the chaotic energy that allows it to evolve in so many different directions.

### Evolution

Mickus can evolve from Minimick when Minimick gains a single level.

Mickus's evolutions are too numerous to enumerate here.

**Previous Evolution:** Minimick (trading)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mick, Mike, Miku, Plantcas, Ickus, Mickachu, Mickey, and many others.  Mickey (Level 60).
