# Mickey

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Bigger Gremlin Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 28.7 lbs (13.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Bigger Gremlin Mickémon. Visually identical to Mickus except slightly larger. Many Trainers regret evolving into this form because its level resets to 1."

### Biology
Mickey is visually nearly identical to Mickus except it is noticeably larger in every proportion. Its eyes are a bit wider, its mullet tuft is fuller, and its hat sits more prominently on its head. Despite the increase in size and the effort required to reach this form, Mickey retains the exact same derpy, chaotic personality as its smaller counterparts. Many Trainers feel a strange mix of pride and mild disappointment upon evolving into Mickey, as it looks like a "premium" Mickus but behaves exactly the same.

### Evolution

Mickey evolves from Mickus at level 60.  Upon evolving into Mickey it returns to level 1.

Mickey can evolve into every other form that Mickus can, except for Minimick.  Mickey will evolve into Bigmick when it reaches level 99.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Level 60)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Bigmick (Level 99), almost any other Mickus evolution.
