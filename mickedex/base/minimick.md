# Minimick

**The Tiny Gremlin Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Tiny Gremlin Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'00" (0.3 m)  
**Weight:** 8.8 lbs (4.0 kg)

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Tiny Gremlin Mickémon. It looks exactly like Mickus but slightly smaller. It is so eager to grow that it evolves back into Mickus after gaining even a single level."

### Biology
Minimick is a very small, slightly shrunken version of Mickus. Every feature - its round potato-shaped body, big sparkling eyes, mullet tuft, and signature hat i is proportionally smaller. Despite its tiny size, it is incredibly energetic and eager to grow. It often runs in frantic circles or tries to copy its Trainer’s movements at high speed, as if trying to prove it deserves to evolve as quickly as possible. Trainers sometimes say Minimick looks like a Mickus that got briefly shrunk by a mischievous Psychic-type.

### Evolution

Minimick can evolve from Mickus during a trade.  If there is no Held Item and the Mickus is not being traded for another Mickus, then there is a 50% chance that upon receiving the Mickus it will instead have evolved into a Minimick.

Upon levelling up one time, Minimick evolves into Mickus.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (trading)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickus (level up once)
