# Lunar Mick

**Type:** Dark / Psychic  
**Classification:** Full Moon Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #097  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Full Moon Mickémon. Only appears during full moons. Its power waxes and wanes with the lunar cycle."

### Biology
Lunar Mick only appears during full moons. Its body glows with a soft silvery light, and it has elegant crescent moon antennae that shimmer in the darkness. Its power waxes and wanes with the lunar cycle, reaching its peak when the moon is fullest. Lunar Mick is mysterious and graceful, often howling beautifully at the night sky or leaving trails of sparkling lunar dust as it moves. It is said to have a mischievous side, occasionally playing harmless pranks under the moonlight.

### Evolution

Lunar Mick evolves from Mickoon when it levels during a Full Moon at Night.
