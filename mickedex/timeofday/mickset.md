# Mickset

**Type:** Psychic / Ice  
**Classification:** Evening Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'07" (1.1 m)  
**Weight:** 66.1 lbs (30.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Evening Mickémon. Full of spite and exhaustion. Its evening cries keeup awake the entire neighborhood."

### Biology
Mickset is a grumpy and exhausted evening Mickus filled with spite and tiredness. Its body seems to absorb the light around it, with little tendrils of shadow framing its head like a halo. It greets each night with loud, mournful cries that keep up its entire neighborhood. Mickset is lacks motivation and tries to get to bed as soon as it can.

### Evolution

Mickset evolves from Mickus if it levels up to 33 or higher at Dusk.
