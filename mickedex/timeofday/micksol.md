# Micksol

**Type:** Fire / Psychic  
**Classification:** Sunny Surfer Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #098  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 77.2 lbs (35.0 kg)

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Sunny Surfer Mickémon. Rides waves of fire and never stops smiling."

### Biology
Micksol is a laid-back, sun-loving Mickus with a permanent golden tan and bright, glowing markings across its body. It wears cool shades made of hardened sunlight and is almost always seen riding waves of fire and psychic energy like a surfboard. Micksol radiates warmth and positive vibes, often leaving a trail of sparkling embers and floating sparkles in its wake. It is known for its carefree attitude and ability to brighten the mood of any Trainer or Mickémon around it.

### Evolution

Micksol evolves from Mickus when a Sun Stone is used on it.  Micksol evolves into Solmick when levelling up during Daytime.

**Previous Evolution:** Minimick, Mickus, Mickey, or Bigmick (Sun Stone)
**Next Evolution(s):** Solmick (Daytime)
