# Mickrowave

**The Microwave Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire / Electric  
**Classification:** Microwave Mickémon  
Height: 4'03" (1.3 m)  
Weight: 154.3 lbs (70.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"Claims its name is pronounced Meecro-wah-vey. It can instantly heat or zap anything with powerful microwave bursts."

### Biology
Mickrowave is a bulky, boxy robotic Mickus whose body has been heavily modified using Mickintosh technology. Its torso resembles a large microwave oven with a glowing glass door in its chest. It has powerful heating coils for arms and can fire concentrated microwave beams that instantly heat or electrocute targets. Despite its intimidating combat ability, it still has the classic Mickus derpiness - it sometimes accidentally pops popcorn mid-battle or overheats and makes a loud "ding!" sound when finished.

Mickrowave claims its name is pronounced Meecro-wah-vey and gets every upset it it's pronounced "Microwave."

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickatron when it defeats a Mickintosh in battle.
