# Mickatron

**Type:** Electric / Steel  
**Classification:** Glitching Robot Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Glitching Robot Mickémon. Overcharged and unstable. Occasionally blue-screens during battle."

### Biology
Mickatron is an overcharged robotic version of Mickachu. Exposed wires form its wild mullet, and its cheeks house massive batteries that constantly spark and arc with electricity. It frequently glitches, twitches, and occasionally blue-screens mid-battle, freezing in place with a distorted "M-M-Mick!" sound. Despite its unstable nature, it is fiercely loyal and becomes extremely powerful when fully charged.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickachu when an Electirizer is used on it.
