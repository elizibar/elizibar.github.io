# Mickachu

**Type:** Electric  
**Classification:** Mullet Rodent Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #041  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Mullet Rodent Mickémon. Its cheeks spark with electricity while its mullet stands on end. It gets extremely offended if anyone calls it cute."

### Biology
Mickachu strongly resembles a standard rodent Mickémon but with a signature messy mullet tuft and a lightning bolt-shaped tail. Its cheeks constantly spark with electricity, and it has a habit of puffing up its mullet when it feels threatened or offended. Mickachu is surprisingly prideful and becomes furious if anyone calls it cute or compares it to other rodent Mickémon. It stores large amounts of electricity in its mullet, which stands on end when fully charged.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickus when a Thunder Stone is used on it.

Evolves into Mickachu X at level 38 or into Mickatron with an Electerizer.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Thunder Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickachu X (Level 38), Mickatron (Electerizer), Mickrowave (Mickintosh), Mickrochip (Mickintosh)
