# Mickrochip

**Type:** Electric/Bug  
**Classification:** Microchip Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #045  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 6.6 lbs (3.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Microchip Mickémon. It uses stolen Mickintosh seeds to hack into electronic systems."

### Biology
Mickrochip is a tiny, circuit-board bug Mickus covered in glowing green circuitry patterns. It is extremely fast and nimble, able to interface with machines by plugging its antennae into them. It uses the advanced tech seeds from Mickintosh to "hack" electronic devices and Mickémon, causing malfunctions, status conditions, or temporary control. Despite being a highly advanced microchip, it still has the derpy Mickus personality and often gets overly excited, causing system errors in whatever it’s trying to hack.

Sometimes says softly "I know this" before executing moves.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickatron after winning a Double Battle on the same side as Mickintosh.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickatron (Mickintosh)
