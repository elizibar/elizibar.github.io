# Realoldmick

**Type:** Normal / Rock  
**Classification:** True Elder Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'06" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:**  19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It refuses to learn anything new and is completely immune to status conditions."

### Biology
Realoldmick is an even older, more stubborn version of Oldmick. Its wrinkles are deeper, its beard is longer and whiter, and it carries two canes now. It has a permanent scowl and refuses to learn any new moves, even from TMs or HMs. Its stubbornness is so extreme that it has become completely immune to all negative status conditions by simply refusing to be affected. It spends its days complaining about "these newfangled moves" and telling the same stories over and over again.

**Ability:** Stubborn Old Man:  Immune to all status conditions. Cannot learn new moves after evolution.

### Evolution
Evolves from Oldmick when it levels while holding an Everstone.

**Previous Evolution:** Oldmick (Everstone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Deadmick (Fainting)
