# Deadmick

**The Deceased Elder Pokémon**

**Type:** Normal / Ghost  
**Classification:** Deceased Elder Pokémon  
**Height:** 1'06" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Deceased Elder Pokémon. It is still complaining, even in death."

### Biology
Deadmick is the ghostly remains of Realoldmick. It is a translucent, floating version of the old Mickus with a permanent scowl and a faint halo of dark energy. It continues to complain about everything from beyond the grave, floating around and muttering about how "back in my day we stayed dead properly." Despite being deceased, it is surprisingly active and can still battle, using ghostly versions of its old moves. It refuses to move on and insists it still has stories to tell.
### Evolution
Evolves when Realoldmick Faints in battle.
