# Mickmickboom

**The Gun Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Normal  
**Classification:** Gun Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'04" (0.4 m)  
**Weight:** 22.0 lbs (10.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Gun Mickémon. Mickmickboom is literally a .50 calibre pistol wearing a tiny hat. It shouts 'MICK BOOM!' before firing."

### Biology
Mickmickboom is exactly what it sounds like, a shiny metallic .50 calibre pistol with a Mickus hat sitting on top of the slide. It has two big cartoon eyes near the rear sight and a tiny mouth that grins maniacally when it fires. Despite being a literal firearm, it is still very much a Mickus at heart: loud, chaotic, and a little bit derpy.

It loves yelling its own name dramatically before shooting ("MIIIIIIICK BOOOOOOM!") and gets visibly disappointed if it misses. It considers itself the coolest member of the digital line and constantly tries to show off by doing trick shots and fancy gun spins (which usually end with the hat falling off).

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon or Mickmonga if it levels while knowing a Steel type move.
