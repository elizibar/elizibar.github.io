# Tormick

**The Torpedo Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Steel  
**Classification:** Torpedo Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"It is shaped like a torpedo and loves launching itself at high speeds, often yelling 'MICK TORPEDO!' before impact."

### Biology
Tormick is literally a living torpedo with a Mickus face on the front, tiny fins, and a mullet-shaped propeller at the back. It has a permanent determined grin and loves charging forward at ridiculous speeds, shouting its own name like a battle cry. It is surprisingly durable and can survive crashing into rocks, boats, and other Mickémon with minimal damage. It still has the classic Mickus derpiness, it often misjudges its trajectory and ends up stuck in the mud or embedded in a wall.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon when it knows both a Water and Steel move.  Evolves into Unterseemick at level 40.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickmon (Water and Steel-type move)
**Next Evolution(s):** Unterseemick (Lv 40)
