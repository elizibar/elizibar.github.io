# Mickeart

**Type:** Normal / Heart  
**Classification:** Heart Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It is shaped like a big, glowing heart and radiates pure love and affection for everything around it."

### Biology
Mickeart is a literal heart-shaped Mickus with big sparkling eyes and tiny arms and legs. It constantly emits pink sparkles and heart-shaped energy waves. It is hopelessly romantic and loves everyone unconditionally. It will try to hug opponents, give flowers to wild Mickémon, and cheer for both sides during battles. Its love is so strong that it can sometimes cause status conditions like Infatuation or heal allies just by existing near them. Despite its pure heart, it is still a Mickus and can be comically oblivious to social cues.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon if it levels while knowing Attract.
