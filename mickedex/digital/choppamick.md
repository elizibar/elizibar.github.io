# Choppamick

**The Attack Helicopter Mickémon**

**Type:** Flying / Steel  
**Classification:** Attack Helicopter Mickémon  
**Height:** 9'10" (3.0 m)  
**Weight:** 1,543.2 lbs (700.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It is a fully armed and operational attack helicopter with a Mickus face on the cockpit. It loves dramatic combat drops and one-liners."

### Biology
Choppamick is a massive, heavily armed attack helicopter with a Mickus face painted on the cockpit glass. It has spinning rotor blades, missile pods, and a minigun turret. Despite being a military-grade machine, it still has the classic Mickus personality, shouting one-liners like "Time to drop in!" and "I’m bringing the boom!" before every attack. It loves dramatic combat insertions and often does unnecessary barrel rolls just because it looks cool. It is surprisingly loyal and protective of its Trainer, even while accidentally blowing up half the battlefield.
