# Mickinabox

**The Fast Food Mascot Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Steel  
**Classification:** Fast Food Mascot Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m) (box form), 4'03" (1.3 m) (sprung form)  
**Weight:** 44.1 lbs (20.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It insists there is a popular fast food chain called Mick-in-a-Box. There is not. It is a jack-in-the-box toy that violently springs out at random."

### Biology
Mickinabox is a colorful, slightly dented jack-in-the-box toy with a big Mickus hat logo on the front. Inside the box is a very enthusiastic Mickus wearing a paper fast food hat and a big plastic smile. It constantly tries to advertise its non-existent fast food chain, shouting slogans like "Come try our world-famous Mick-in-a-Box Meal!" and offering imaginary combo deals.

When excited, or when it feels like it, it violently springs out of the box with a loud BOING, often scaring anyone nearby. It then immediately tries to hand out coupons for a restaurant that does not exist. Mickinabox is convinced its chain is extremely popular and gets genuinely confused when people say they’ve never heard of it.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon if it levels while holding a Strange Coupon.
