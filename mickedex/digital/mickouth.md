# Mickouth

**The Mouth Mickémon**

**Type:** Fairy  
**Classification:** Mouth Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It is a giant floating mouth that never stops talking, commentating on everything happening around it."

### Biology
Mickouth is essentially a giant, floating Mickus mouth with tiny arms and legs. It never stops talking, providing running commentary on battles, the weather, what other Mickémon are doing, and its own thoughts. Its voice is loud, slightly distorted, and it has opinions on everything. It can fire sound waves and sonic blasts from its mouth, but it mostly just uses it to talk nonstop. It is surprisingly good at hyping up its allies with constant encouragement, even if it never shuts up.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon if it levels while knowing Alluring Voice.
