# Helimick

**Type:** Flying / Steel  
**Classification:** Propeller Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Propeller Mickémon. A tiny propeller on its hat lets it hover and fly in erratic patterns."

### Biology
Helimick is a Mickus with a comically small propeller attached to the top of its hat. The propeller spins rapidly, allowing it to hover and fly in wobbly, unpredictable patterns. It has a permanent look of concentration mixed with mild panic as it tries to steer itself. It loves doing aerial tricks but often loses control and crashes into trees, buildings, or other Mickémon. Despite its clumsy flying, it is very proud of its "air superiority" and challenges grounded Mickémon to races.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon when it levels while knowing both a Flying and Steel-type move. Evolves into Choppamick at level 45.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickmon (Flying and Steel-type moves)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Choppamick (Level 45)
