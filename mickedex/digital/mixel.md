# Mixel

**Type:** Fairy  
**Classification:** Glitch Sprite Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #070  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m) (varies)  
**Weight:** 22.0 lbs (10.0 kg) (varies)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"Its form is so unstable that it looks completely different every time it is sent out of its Poké Ball. It exists in a constant state of pixelated delirium."

### Biology
Mixel is a wildly unstable 16-bit SNES-era sprite Mickus. Its body is made of chunky, brightly colored pixels that constantly flicker, shift, and corrupt. Every time it is released from its Pokéball, it loads a different sprite - sometimes a cute chibi version, sometimes a horrifying glitched nightmare, sometimes a bizarre hybrid of other Mickus forms.

It speaks in distorted 16-bit sound bites and has a habit of randomly changing its attack animations mid-battle. Mixel is cheerful, deranged, and completely unpredictable. It seems to treat reality like a poorly coded game and finds great joy in breaking it.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmonga if Mickmonga levels up while holding a Fairy Feather or if it wins a battle with a Fairy-type move.
