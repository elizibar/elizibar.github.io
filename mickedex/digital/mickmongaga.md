# Mickmongaga

**The Big Sword Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Psychic  
**Classification:** Big Sword Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'11" (1.8 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"It believes it comes from a small peaceful town, but it is actually absorbing memories from other Mickus. It wields an absurdly large sword it can barely lift."

### Biology
Mickmongaga is a tall, spiky-haired Mickus wearing a sleeveless vest and baggy pants, with a comically oversized sword strapped to its back that’s almost as tall as it is. It has a serious, brooding expression that is constantly undermined by its inherent Mickus derpiness. It frequently trips over its own sword or gets the blade stuck in the ground.

It speaks in dramatic one-liners and constantly talks about ""he small town where I grew up," despite clear evidence that it was born in a lab or a Poké Ball. In reality, it is unconsciously absorbing fragmented memories from other Mickus (especially those who have been traded or traveled far). It believes these second-hand memories are its own and will passionately defend the fictional hometown it has never actually visited.

Despite the giant sword being far too heavy for it, Mickmongaga insists on using it in every battle, often spinning around uncontrollably after swinging it.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmonga if Mickmonga levels up while knowing both a Fairy-type move and a Steel-type move.
