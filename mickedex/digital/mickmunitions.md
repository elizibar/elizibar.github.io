# Mickmunitions

**The Action Hero Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Dark  
**Classification:** Action Hero Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 187.4 lbs (85.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Action Hero Mickémon. It drops one-liners constantly while dual-wielding ridiculous amounts of weaponry."

### Biology
Mickmunitions is a walking action movie stereotype. It wears cool shades, a red headband, a sleeveless tactical vest packed with grenades, and has knives and guns strapped to every available surface. Its mullet flows dramatically in the wind (even indoors). It constantly drops one-liners like "Yippie ki-yay," "I’m too old for this," and "Let’s rock" at every possible opportunity, including when it trips over its own grenades or accidentally shoots itself in the foot.

Despite looking like it could solo an army, it is still very much a Mickus at heart: loud, chaotic, and prone to slapstick. It loves dramatic slow-motion poses and explosions, even when there’s no reason for either.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickmon when it participates in a Double Battle with Mickmickboom and wins.
