# Unterseemick

**Type:** Water / Steel  
**Classification:** Submarine Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #077  
**Height:** 8'02" (2.5 m)  
**Weight:** 771.6 lbs (350.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It is a fully functional submarine with a Mickus face on the conning tower. It insists it is 'going deeper' even in shallow water."

### Biology
Unterseemick is a massive, fully functional submarine shaped like a Mickus. It has a cartoonish Mickus face on the sail (conning tower), tiny arms that operate the periscope, and a mullet-shaped radar mast. It constantly makes dramatic diving sounds and insists it is on important underwater missions, even when it’s just in a kiddie pool. It can fire bubble torpedoes and has surprisingly strong sonar that it uses mostly to find shiny objects on the bottom. Like all Mickus, it is still very derpy. It frequently gets stuck in shallow water or accidentally surfaces under boats.
