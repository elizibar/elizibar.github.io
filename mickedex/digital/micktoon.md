# Micktoon

**The Indie Cartoon Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Fairy  
**Classification:** Indie Cartoon Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m) (constantly shifting)  
**Weight:** 11.0 lbs (5.0 kg) (varies wildly)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"Its body is made of sketchy, shifting lines and it moves with a janky, low-budget framerate. It is completely unpredictable."

### Biology
Micktoon is a deranged, hand-drawn version of Mickmon that looks like it crawled straight out of a fever-dream indie cartoon from the early 2010s. Its lines are rough and constantly shifting, its colors bleed outside the lines, and it moves with a choppy, low-framerate animation style that makes it look like it's glitching between keyframes.

One moment it has beautiful smooth animation, the next it's a scribbled mess with extra limbs or missing eyes. It speaks in warped, pitch-shifting voice clips and has a habit of breaking the fourth wall by looking directly at the Trainer and waving. Micktoon is energetic, unhinged, and seems to treat reality like a poorly animated cartoon it can redraw at any moment.
