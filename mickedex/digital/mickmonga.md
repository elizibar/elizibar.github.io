# Mickmonga

**The Armored Digimon Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel  
**Classification:** Armored Digimon Mickémon  
**Height:** 8'02" (2.5 m)  
**Weight:** 771.6 lbs (350.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Armored Digimon Mickémon. Announces every attack with maximum drama."

### Biology
Mickmonga is a massive, heavily armored evolution of Mickmon. It has enormous shoulder pads, glowing barcode markings across its chest, and a dramatic digital cape. It announces every single attack with maximum drama, even the weakest ones. Mickmonga takes itself very seriously and behaves like a final boss from a 90s RPG, despite still being fundamentally a derpy Mickus at heart.

### Evolution

Mickmonga evolves from Mickmon at level 42.
