# Mickmelt

**Type:** Ice / Water  
**Classification:** Melting Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 22.0 lbs (10.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Melting Mickémon. It is slowly melting at all times and is very self-conscious about it."

### Biology
Mickmelt is a Mickcube that has begun to melt. It is a lopsided, dripping ice cube with a worried expression and a hat that is slightly askew. Water constantly drips from its body, leaving a trail behind it. It is very aware that it is melting and is strangely self-conscious about the process, often trying (and failing) to hold itself together. Despite its gradual disintegration, it remains surprisingly cheerful and determined, even as it slowly becomes a puddle.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickcube if it learns a Fire-type move and levels up.
