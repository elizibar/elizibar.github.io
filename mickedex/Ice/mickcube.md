# Mickcube

**Type:** Ice  
**Classification:** Ice Cube Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Ice Cube Mickémon. It is literally just a perfectly square ice cube with a hat on top."

### Biology
Mickcube is exactly what it sounds like, a perfectly square block of ice with a tiny Mickus hat sitting on top. It has two big, expressive eyes and a small mouth that appears on its front face. Despite having no limbs, it moves by wiggling and hopping around. It is surprisingly durable for a block of ice and seems quite proud of its simple, minimalist form. It gets visibly annoyed when other Mickémon try to use it as a drink cooler.

### Evolution

Mickcube evolves from Mickool if it levels while holding a Never-Melting Ice.  Evolves into Mickmelt if it learns a Fire-type move.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickool (Never-Melting Ice)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickmelt (Fire-type Move)
