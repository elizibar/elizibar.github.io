# Mickooler

**Type:** Ice  
**Classification:** Even Cooler Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 130.1 lbs (59.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Even Cooler Mickémon. Claims to have reached peak coolness. Still slips on its own ice."

### Biology
Mickooler believes it has reached the absolute peak of coolness. Its ice sunglasses are larger, its frost trails are more dramatic, and its poses are even more exaggerated. It claims to have achieved "maximum chill." However, it still slips on its own ice more often than it cares to admit. Mickooler is slightly more confident (and slightly more clumsy) than Mickool.

### Evolution

Mickooler evolves from Mickool at level 33.
