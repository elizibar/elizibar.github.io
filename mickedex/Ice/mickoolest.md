# Mickoolest

**The Ultimate Cool Mickémon**

**Type:** Ice  
**Classification:** Ultimate Cool Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'11" (1.5 m)  
**Weight:** 143.3 lbs (65.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Ultimate Cool Mickémon. It has achieved the absolute peak of coolness and refuses to acknowledge anything less than legendary levels of chill."

### Biology
Mickoolest is the final form of the Mickool line and believes it has reached the absolute pinnacle of coolness. It wears the largest, most stylish ice sunglasses in existence, has a perfectly sculpted icy mullet, and carries itself with an air of effortless superiority. Its body is covered in pristine, crystalline ice that never melts, and it leaves a dramatic trail of frost and sparkles wherever it goes.

Mickoolest speaks in a smooth, laid-back tone and constantly strikes overly cool poses. It claims nothing can faze it, though it still occasionally slips on its own ice and tries to play it off like it was intentional. It is the self-proclaimed "coolest Mickémon in the world" and will challenge anyone who disputes that title.

### Evolution

Mickooler evolves from Mickooler at level 60.
