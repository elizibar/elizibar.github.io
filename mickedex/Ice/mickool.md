# Mickool

**The Cool Dude Mickémon**

**Type:** Ice  
**Classification:** Cool Dude Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 121.3 lbs (55.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Cool Dude Mickémon. Wears ice sunglasses. Thinks it’s way smoother than it actually is."

### Biology
Mickool is a self-proclaimed "cool dude" Mickus covered in ice. It wears stylish ice sunglasses and constantly strikes cool poses. It leaves a trail of frost behind it and acts as if it is the smoothest Mickémon alive. In practice, it frequently slips on its own ice and has to play it off like it was intentional. It is convinced its chill vibes are unmatched.

### Evolution

Mickool evolves from Mickus when an Ice Stone is used on it.  Mickool evolves into Mickooler at level 33.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Ice Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickooler (Level 33)
