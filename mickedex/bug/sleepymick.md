# Sleepymick

**Type:** Bug  
**Classification:** Sleeping Hat Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #034  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 14.3 lbs (6.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Sleeping Hat Mickémon. The large hat is the main body. The worm underneath is having the nap of its life."

### Biology
Sleepymick consists almost entirely of an oversized Mickus hat, with a Mickworm peacefully sleeping underneath it. The hat itself appears to be the main body. Sleepymick is almost always napping and is notoriously difficult to wake up, even in the middle of battles. It occasionally lets out soft, contented snoring sounds that can strangely soothe nearby allies.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickworm at level 20 or with High Friendship.  Evolves into Mickly, Mickroach, or Mickbug depending on conditions and also can create a Mickhusk if it evolves with an extra open spot in the party.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickworm (Level 20 or high Friendship)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickfly, Mickroach, Mickbug, or Mickhusk (depending on conditions)
