# Mickroach

**The Indestructible Mickémon**

**Type:** Bug / Dark  
**Classification:** Indestructible Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 29.8 lbs (13.5 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Indestructible Mickémon. Its shell is a hardened hat. It simply refuses to stay squished."

### Biology
Mickroach is an incredibly resilient Bug-type Mickus. Its back is a hardened Mickus hat that functions as an almost indestructible shell. No matter how many times it is knocked down, stomped on, or blasted, Mickroach simply gets back up and keeps going. It has a mischievous personality and seems to enjoy surviving situations that should be impossible. Other Mickémon find its unkillable nature both impressive and slightly disturbing.

### Evolution

Mickroach evolves from Sleepymick when it levels up in a Cave.
