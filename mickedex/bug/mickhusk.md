# Mickhusk

**The Empty Hat Mickémon**

**Type:** Bug / Ghost  
**Classification:** Empty Hat Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'10" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 4.4 lbs (2.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Empty Hat Mickémon. Only the dried-out hat remains. It floats around looking melancholic."

### Biology
Mickhusk is all that remains after Sleepymick’s body disappears: just a dried-out, haunted hat that floats sadly through the air. A single eye is visible inside the brim, and it possesses the rare ability to survive with only 1 HP. Mickhusk feels lonely without its sleepy occupant and drifts around melancholically, occasionally letting out soft, ghostly "mick…" sounds.

### Evolution

When a Sleepymick evolves, it sheds its old hat as it grows into its new form.  That cast-off hat will, if there is room in the party, join the trainer's group as a Mickhusk.  At level 35, or if the Mickhusk possesses a 'high' Attack stat, it will evolve into Mickhusky.

**Previous Evolution:** Sleepymick  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickhusky (Level 35 or high attack stat)
