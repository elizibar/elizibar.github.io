# Mickhusky

**Type:** Ghost / Fighting  
**Classification:** Haunted Bodybuilder Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #039  
**Height:** 5'11" (1.8 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Haunted Bodybuilder Mickémon. Massive muscular limbs burst from an old hat. It flexes even while invisible."

### Biology
Mickhusky is a bizarre and powerful Ghost/Fighting Mickémon. Two massive, muscular arms and two powerful legs burst out from an old haunted hat, creating a ghostly bodybuilder. Despite its intimidating and demonic appearance, it is surprisingly cheerful and loves flexing, even while invisible. It is known for its incredible strength and its habit of striking bodybuilder poses after victories.

### Evolution

Mickhusky evolves from Mickhusk either at level 35 or when it has a 'high' Attack stat.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickhusk (Level 35 or high Attack)  
**Next Evolution(s):** -
