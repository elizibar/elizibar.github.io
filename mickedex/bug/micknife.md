# Micknife

**Type:** Steel/Dark  
**Classification:** Knife Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 1'11" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Sleeping Hat Mickémon. The large hat is the main body. The worm underneath is having the nap of its life."

### Biology
Micknife is a sleek, dapper assassin bug with razor-sharp steel blades for arms. It wears sharp black glasses, a tiny formal vest, and moves with an unsettlingly smooth, theatrical swagger. Its blades are always impeccably clean and polished.

True to its namesake, Micknife is charismatic and theatrical. It often hums or whistles the tune of "Mack the Knife" before striking. It loves making dramatic entrances, appearing from shadows with a flourish, and delivering one-liners in a smooth voice before attacking. Despite being genuinely dangerous, it still has the underlying Mickus derpiness, occasionally getting its blades stuck in the ground mid-pose or tripping over its own dramatic cape flourish.
