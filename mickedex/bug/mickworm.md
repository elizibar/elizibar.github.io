# Mickworm

**The Caterpillar Mickémon**

**Type:** Bug  
**Classification:** Caterpillar Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'00" (0.3 m)  
**Weight:** 6.6 lbs (3.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Caterpillar Mickémon. A simple worm with a mullet. It dreams of hats."

### Biology
Mickworm is a simple, derpy caterpillar Mickémon with a tiny mullet tuft poking out of its head. It spends most of its time slowly crawling around and daydreaming about one day wearing a really cool hat. Despite its unassuming appearance, it is surprisingly resilient and patient, knowing that evolution into Sleepymick (and eventually greater forms) is just a matter of time and naps.

### Evolution

Mickworm is created by using a Leaf Stone on Grossicus.  At level 20, or when it has high Friendship it will evolve into Sleepymick.

**Previous Evolution:** Spawned from Grossicus (Leaf Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Sleepymick (Level 20 or high Friendship)
