# Mickfly

**The Time Traveler Mickémon**

**Type:** Bug / Flying  
**Classification:** Time Traveler Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 49.6 lbs (22.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Time Traveler Mickémon. Wears glasses and a red vest while riding a tiny hoverboard. Insists it is 'totally radical' despite being a massive nerd."

### Biology
Mickfly is a nerdy yet extremely confident Bug/Flying Mickémon. It wears round glasses, a signature red puffy vest, and rides a tiny hoverboard. Despite being a massive nerd who makes "Great Scott!" exclamations, it genuinely believes it is the coolest Mickémon alive. It is obsessed with speed and is constantly trying to go faster, often overshooting its target and crashing dramatically.

### Evolution

Mickfly evolves from Sleepymick if it levels during the Daytime or othewise in an open area.
