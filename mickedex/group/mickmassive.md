# Mickmassive

**The Colony Mickémon**

**Type:** Fighting  
**Classification:** Colony Mickémon  
**Height:** 9'10" (3.0 m) (Varies)  
**Weight:** 1,543.2 lbs (700.0 kg) (Varies)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Colony Mickémon. Dozens of muscular arms and legs stick out of the hat mountain. Surprisingly good at group hugs."

### Biology
Mickmassive is an absolute behemoth formed from the hats of Mickmasses after consuming a Protein Shake. Dozens of muscular arms and powerful legs protrude at random angles from the towering mountain of hats. It looks like a colony of Mickus that decided to hit the gym extremely hard. Despite its intimidating size and chaotic appearance, it is surprisingly good at group hugs and coordinated attacks using its many limbs.
