# Mickoral

**Type:** Water / Fairy  
**Classification:** Glowing Coral Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'11" (1.5 m)  
**Weight:** 220.5 lbs (100.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Glowing Coral Mickémon. Its glowing coral has powerful healing properties."

### Biology
Mickoral is a beautiful, glowing coral colony shaped like a Mickus hat. Its coral structures emit soft, rainbow-colored light that has strong healing properties. It is gentle and nurturing, often creating safe glowing reefs where injured Mickémon can rest. Its light can soothe status conditions and heal allies. Like Mickreef, it is protective of the tiny creatures that live in its body.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickrorganism when it wins a battle in water while holding a Fairy Feather.
