# Mickreef

**The Coral Reef Mickémon Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Grass  
**Classification:** Coral Reef Mickémon Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Coral Reef Mickémon. It builds beautiful coral structures from its own body."

### Biology
Mickreef is a living coral reef colony shaped like a massive, colorful Mickus hat. Tiny colorful coral structures grow all over its body, creating a vibrant underwater ecosystem. It is a master builder and constantly expands its own body into intricate coral formations. It is peaceful and protective of smaller Mickémon that live in its reef body, but it can also weaponize its coral spikes when threatened.

### Evolution

Evolves from Mickrorganiasm when it levels up after winning a battle in water while holding a Leaf Stone.
