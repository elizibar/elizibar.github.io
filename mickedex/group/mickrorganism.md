# Mickrorganism

**Type:** Poison  
**Classification:** Colony Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 11.0 lbs (5.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"It is a living colony of tiny hat-shaped Mickus growing together into a larger hat-shaped organism."

### Biology
Mickorganism is a bizarre, microscopic-scale colony of tiny hat-shaped Mickus that have fused together into a larger, hat-shaped mass. Each individual micro-Mickus is still conscious and can be seen wiggling around inside the colony. The whole organism moves as one, but you can see hundreds of tiny eyes and mouths blinking and chattering.

It constantly grows new micro-Mickus and absorbs nutrients, mostly from spilled soda and snack crumbs.  It constantly grows new micro-Mickus and absorbs nutrients (mostly from spilled soda and snack crumbs). It has a strong Poison typing due to the chaotic mixture of chemicals and biology happening inside the colony. Despite being a living biological disaster, it seems strangely happy and curious about the world.

Evolves from Mickmasses when it's shrunken in a SCIENCE Laboratory.
