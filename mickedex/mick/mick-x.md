# Mick X

**Type:** Fighting / Rock  
**Classification:** Extreme Wrestler Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 264.6 lbs (120.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Extreme Wrestler Mickémon. It constantly yells 'X-TREME!' and performs suplexes on anything that moves. Its muscles have muscles."

### Biology
Mick X is a hyper-muscular, extremely enthusiastic wrestler Mickus. Its body is covered in defined muscles, and it wears tiny wrestling boots and wristbands. It constantly flexes, poses, and yells "X-TREME!" at the top of its lungs. Mick X lives for dramatic suplexes and over-the-top battles. Despite its intimidating physique, it still retains the core derpy charm of the Mickus line and gets surprisingly emotional when it loses.
