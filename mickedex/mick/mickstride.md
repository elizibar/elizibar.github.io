# Mickstride

**The Jogger Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Fighting  
**Classification:** Jogger Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 121.3 lbs (55.0 kg)

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Jogger Mickémon. Always seen power-walking with a sweatband. It claims walking 1,000 steps builds character… and then immediately collapses."

### Biology
Mickstride is an athletic Mickus variant that wears a bright sweatband and tiny running shoes. It is constantly power-walking or jogging in place, even during battles. While it claims that walking builds character, it often dramatically collapses after finishing a long run, only to get back up moments later.

### Evolution

Mickstride evolves from Mickus after walking 1,000 steps while holding a Sweatband.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus  
**Next Evolution(s):** -
