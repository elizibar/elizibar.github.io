# Mick

**Type:** Normal / Rock  
**Classification:** Rockstar Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'11" (1.5 m)  
**Weight:** 187.4 lbs (85.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Rockstar Mickémon. It wears a leather jacket and carries an air guitar. Its powerful screams can trigger earthquakes and spontaneous sing-alongs."

### Biology
Mick is a bipedal, confident evolution of Mickus. It has traded its round potato shape for a taller, more upright posture and now wears a spiked leather jacket that it treats with great pride. Its mullet has been styled into a dramatic rockstar mane. While it projects an aura of coolness and constantly performs air guitar solos, Mick is secretly quite emotional and can become teary-eyed when it hears slow ballads or heartfelt music.

### Evolution

Mick evolves from Mickus when it has High Friendship with its trainer.  It evolves into Mick X at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (High Friendship)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mick X (Level 38)
