# Mickjitsu

**The Martial Arts Master Mickémon**

**Type:** Fighting / Dark  
**Classification:** Martial Arts Master Mickémon  
Height: 5'11" (1.8 m)  
Weight: 231.5 lbs (105.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Martial Arts Master Mickémon. Mickjitsu is a legendary fighter who has mastered many styles. It has an undying love for Nanab Berry sandwiches and is known to meditate for hours while eating them."

### Biology
Mickjitsu is the final evolution of the Micktorious line. It has fully embraced its destiny as a martial arts master. It wears a black gi with gold trim, a championship belt, and still sports an extravagant pompadour mullet. Its movements are fluid and powerful, blending showmanship with genuine fighting skill.

Despite achieving true mastery, Mickjitsu remains incredibly extra, it yells "Thank you! Thank you very much!" after landing powerful techniques and constantly tries to look cool. It has an obsessive love for Nanab Berry sandwiches, often carrying several with it and offering them to worthy opponents as a sign of respect (or eating them mid-battle when it gets hungry).

### Evolution

Mickjitsu evolves from Micktorious at level 38.
