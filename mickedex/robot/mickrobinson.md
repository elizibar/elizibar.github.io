# Mickbot

**The Retro Robot Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Psychic  
**Classification:** Retro Robot Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 352.7 lbs (160.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Retro Robot Mickémon. It constantly shouts "Danger! Danger!" in a very dramatic voice."

### Biology
Mickrobinson is a tall, boxy 1960s-style robot with flashing lights, antenna ears, and a shiny silver finish. It moves with stiff, jerky motions and has a very serious, overly dramatic personality. It constantly warns everyone about "danger" even when there isn’t any. Its voice is deep and robotic, and it loves giving long, unnecessary safety lectures. Despite being outdated, it is surprisingly powerful and protective of its Trainer.

### Evolution

Evolves frrom Mickro or Mickbot when it knows 3 moves that are 25+ levels old.
