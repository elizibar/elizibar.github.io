# Mickro

**The Vista Robot Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel  
**Classification:** Vista Robot Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'11" (1.2 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Vista Robot Mickémon. This outdated robot Mickus still runs on Windows Vista. It frequently freezes mid-battle."

### Biology
Mickro is a clunky, boxy robot version of Mickus still running on very outdated software. Its body is made of dull metal plating with exposed wires forming its mullet, and its screen face occasionally flickers or displays error messages. It speaks in distorted robotic beeps and frequently freezes mid-action with a sad "Miiiick…" sound. Despite its technical issues, Mickro is strangely proud of its vintage technology.

### Evolution

Mickro evolves from Mickus when traded while holding a Metal Coat.  Mickro evolves into Mickbot at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Trade with Metal Coat)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickbot (Level 38)
