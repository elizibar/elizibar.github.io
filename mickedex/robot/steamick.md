# Steamick

**Type:** Fire / Steel  
**Classification:** Steam-Powered Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'11" (1.5 m)  
**Weight:** 264.6 lbs (120.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Steam-Powered Mickémon. It runs on old-fashioned steam power and is very loud."

### Biology
Steamick is a bulky, brass-and-iron Victorian-style robot powered by a massive boiler on its back that constantly belches steam. It has pressure gauges, pipes, and a big smokestack. It is incredibly loud and every movement is accompanied by hissing steam and clanking metal. Steamick is surprisingly strong but tends to overheat dramatically during long battles, sometimes shooting jets of steam from its joints in excitement.

### Evolution

Evolves when Mickro or Mickbot Faints as the result of a Fire-type move.
