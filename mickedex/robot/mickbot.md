# Mickbot

**Type:** Steel  
**Classification:** Upgraded Robot Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 209.4 lbs (95.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Upgraded Robot Mickémon. An improved version of Mickro with slightly better graphics."

### Biology
Mickbot is a sleeker, upgraded version of Mickro. While still clearly robotic, it has smoother plating, brighter LED eyes, and fewer exposed wires. It proudly claims to be "state of the art... from 2007" and often boasts about its improved graphics. However, it still runs on questionable software and occasionally freezes mid-battle with a distorted "Miiiiick…" sound. Mickbot is strangely proud of its outdated technology and refuses to accept any firmware updates.

### Evolution

Mickbot evolves from Mickro at level 38.
