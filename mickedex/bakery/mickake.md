# Mickake

**The Cake Mickémon**

**Type:** Fairy / Fire  
**Classification:** Cake Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Cake Pokémon. It is a living cake that loves baking elaborate cakes for every occasion."

### Biology
Mickake is a living, multi-layered cake with fluffy frosting, decorative icing flowers, and a cherry on top. Its "body" is soft sponge cake with a warm, sweet scent. It has tiny arms and legs, a chef’s hat, and its signature mullet made of frosting swirls. It is a passionate baker that specializes in elaborate cakes for birthdays, weddings, and celebrations. It takes great pride in its work and gets genuinely emotional when people enjoy its creations. In battle, it can fire frosting blasts or use its sweet aroma to calm opponents.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Angel of Cakes.
