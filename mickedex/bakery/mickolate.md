# Mickolate

**The Chocolate Rabbit Mickémon**

**Type:** Fairy / Fire  
**Classification:** Chocolate Rabbit Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 19.8 lbs (9.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Chocolate Rabbit Pokémon. It is a master chocolatier that makes delicious confections and brews perfect mochas and lattes."

### Biology
Mickolate is an adorable chocolate rabbit Mickus with glossy brown "fur" made of smooth chocolate, long floppy ears, and a tiny chef’s hat. It carries a small tray of confections and a steaming mug of coffee. It is incredibly skilled at making chocolates, truffles, cakes, and perfect coffee drinks (especially mochas and lattes). Its chocolate is always fresh and high-quality, and its coffee is said to have energizing and slightly magical properties. It has a gentle, sweet personality and loves sharing its creations with friends. In battle, it uses chocolatey blasts and steaming coffee attacks with surprising effectiveness.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Angel of Cakes.
