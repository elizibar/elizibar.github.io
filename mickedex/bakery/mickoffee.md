# Mickoffee

**The Caffeinated Mickémon**

**Type:** Electric / Normal  
**Classification:** Caffeinated Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Caffeinated Pokémon. It is a hyper, jittery speedster powered by endless coffee."

### Biology
Mickoffee is a hyperactive, jittery Mickus with wide, bloodshot eyes and a permanent coffee-stained mullet. It wears a tiny barista apron and has steam constantly rising from its head. It moves at blinding speeds, vibrating with caffeine energy and leaving a trail of coffee droplets behind it. It talks extremely fast and has trouble staying still, often running in circles or bouncing off walls. Despite its jittery nature, it is incredibly fast and can deliver quick electric shocks or rapid punches. It is addicted to coffee and gets extremely cranky (and slower) if it doesn’t get its fix.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Angel of Cakes.
