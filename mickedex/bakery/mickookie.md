# Mickookie

**The Cookie Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Fairy  
**Classification:** Cookie Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Cookie Mickémon.	 It is a living cookie that loves baking all kinds of cookies and no-bakes, from chocolate chip to peanut butter oatmeal."

### Biology
Mickoockie is a living, golden-brown chocolate chip cookie with a cheerful Mickus face, tiny arms and legs, and a chef’s hat. Its body is soft and warm, with gooey chocolate chips that smell absolutely delicious. It is a passionate baker that spends most of its time in the kitchen, whipping up batches of chocolate chip, sugar, oatmeal raisin, and no-bake cookies. It loves sharing its creations with friends and gets genuinely happy when people enjoy them. In battle, it can fire cookie crumbs or use its warm, comforting presence to calm opponents.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Angel of Cakes.
