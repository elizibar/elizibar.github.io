# Mipie

**The Pie Mickémon**

**Type:** Fairy / Grass  
**Classification:** Pie Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 15.4 lbs (7.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Pie Mickémon. It is a living pie that bakes delicious pies, custards, tarts, and other sweet goods."

### Biology
Mipie is a living, golden-crusted pie with a flaky, buttery crust and a variety of fillings (apple, cherry, pecan, etc.). It has tiny arms and legs, a chef’s hat, and its signature mullet tuft made of decorative lattice crust. It is a passionate baker that specializes in pies, custards, tarts, and other sweet pastries. It takes great pride in its work and loves sharing its creations with friends and Trainers. In battle, it can fire bursts of warm filling or use its flaky crust as a shield. It has a warm, comforting personality and is known for its ability to calm down agitated Mickémon with the smell of fresh-baked pie.

### Evolution
Evolves from Mickus when trained by the Angel of Cakes.
