# Mickmarried

**The Union Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Fairy  
**Classification:** Union Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 209.4 lbs (95.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Union Mickémon. Formed from Sir Mick and Lady Miku’s bond. Its vows grant it exceptional strength."

### Biology
Mickmarried is a fusion of Sir Mick and Lady Miku, representing their union. The top half wears a tiny elegant tuxedo jacket with a mullet, while the bottom half flows into a graceful wedding dress with water-based twin-tails. It radiates warmth, love, and stability. Its bond is so strong that it gains significantly improved durability and supportive abilities. Mickmarried is often seen gently supporting allies in battle while radiating a soft, loving glow.

### Evolution

When Sir Mick and Lady Miku are in the same party together and one levels up, the two merge into Mickmarried (which inherits the level of the higher of the pair).
