# Sir Mick

**The Knight Mickémon**

**Type:** Steel / Fighting  
**Classification:** Knight Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Knight Mickémon. A gallant mullet knight in shining armor."

### Biology
Sir Mick is a gallant, knightly Mickus clad in shining armor with its mullet flowing out from under a helmet. It carries a small sword and shield and behaves with chivalrous (if occasionally clumsy) honor. It is extremely protective of Lady Miku and takes its role as a knight very seriously, often attempting dramatic rescues or standing guard dramatically even when nothing is threatening anyone.

### Evolution

Evolves from a Male Mickus when a Dawn Stone is used on it.  Evolves into Mickmarried if it levels while teaming with Lady Miku, both will merge into a single Mickmarried.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Dawn Stone, Male)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickmarried (with Lady Miku)
