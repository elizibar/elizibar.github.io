# Mickfambly

**Type:** Steel / Fairy  
**Classification:** Family Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 5'07" (1.7 m)  
**Weight:** 264.6 lbs (120.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Family Mickémon. It consists of a Mickmarried with two small Mickus children orbiting around it."

### Biology
Mickfambly is a loving family unit: the main Mickmarried body is now accompanied by two small, floating Mickus "children" that orbit around it like Dugtrio heads. The parents still wear their wedding attire, while the kids wear tiny versions of their parents’ outfits. The whole family works together in battle: the parents provide strong defensive support while the kids zip around delivering fast attacks and healing moves. They are incredibly wholesome and protective of each other, often posing dramatically as a family after winning battles.

The name is intentionally misspelled because the kids kept trying to write "family" on everything and this is how it came out.

Evolves from Mickmarried at level 50.
