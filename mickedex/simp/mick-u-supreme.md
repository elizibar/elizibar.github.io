# Mick U Supreme

**The Ultimate Simp Mickémon**

**Type:** Psychic / Heart  
**Classification:** Ultimate Simp Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 15.4 lbs (7.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Ultimate Simp Mickémon. Has achieved nirvana through unlimited gift-giving."

### Biology
Mick U Supreme has reached the pinnacle of simp enlightenment. Its eyes are permanently heart-shaped, and it floats slightly off the ground due to pure devotion. It has achieved an almost zen-like state through unlimited gift-giving and will move mountains (sometimes literally) for its Trainer. Despite its immense power, it still gets flustered and blushes intensely whenever its Trainer shows it even the smallest amount of attention.

### Evolution

Mick U Supreme evolves from Mick U when it has many items used on it out of combat.
