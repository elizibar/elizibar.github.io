# Mickflix

**The Binge-Watcher Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal / Psychic  
**Classification:** Binge-Watcher Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Binge-Watcher Mickémon. It has achieved peak laziness and spends all day watching shows while wrapped in blankets."
### Biology
Mickflix is a lazy, blob-like Mickus buried in a massive pile of blankets and snack wrappers. Its eyes are half-lidded from constant screen time, and it has a glowing remote control floating nearby. It uses Psychic powers to levitate snacks and change channels without moving. Despite its extreme laziness, it has surprisingly strong Psychic abilities from all the "mental exercise" of judging plot twists. It gets genuinely upset when someone interrupts its binge sessions.
