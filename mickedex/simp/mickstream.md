# Mickstream

**Type:** Water  
**Classification:** Live-Streamer Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #093  
**Height:** 4'03" (1.3 m)  
**Weight:** 77.2 lbs (35.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Live-Streamer Mickémon. It wants more attention than its Trainer can give, so it started streaming its own life 24/7."
### Biology
Mickstream is a flashy, attention-hungry Mickus with glowing digital water effects and a permanent "live" indicator floating above its head. It carries a floating camera drone and is constantly streaming everything it does: battles, training, eating snacks, even sleeping. It has a very online personality, uses internet slang, and gets visibly upset if its viewer count drops. Its water powers are now mixed with digital effects, allowing it to create holographic chat messages and emotes during battle.
