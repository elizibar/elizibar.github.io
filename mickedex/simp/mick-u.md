# Mick U

**The Simp Mickémon**

**Type:** Psychic / Heart  
**Classification:** Simp Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Simp Mickémon. Blushes furiously. Its power grows the more its Trainer spoils it."

### Biology
Mick U is a hopelessly lovestruck Mickus with permanent heart-shaped pupils and a bright red blush across its face. It becomes stronger the more its Trainer spoils it with gifts, attention, and affection. Mick U will do almost anything to please its Trainer, including carrying items, cheering excessively in battle, or attempting (and usually failing) dramatic rescue attempts. Its devotion is both endearing and slightly concerning.

### Evolution

Mick U evolves from Mickus when a Love Stone is used on it.  It evolves to Mick U Supreme when many items are given to it out of combat.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Love Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mick U Supreme
