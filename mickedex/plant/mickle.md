# Mickle

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Sour Pickle Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'01" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 22.0 lbs (10.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Sour Pickle Mickémon. Mickle is a grumpy, cucumber-shaped fighter that evolved from Plantcas or Ickus. It is perpetually sour and loves picking fights while complaining about everything."

### Biology
Mickle is a sour, pickle-shaped Mickus with a permanently puckered expression. Its body is a shiny dark green cucumber with bumpy "warts" and a leafy tuft on top that resembles a mullet gone wrong. It wears tiny boxing gloves made from hardened seeds and is always ready to throw hands (or pickles).

True to its name, Mickle is incredibly sour, both in personality and literally. It constantly complains about the weather, other Mickémon, its opponents, and the general state of the world. Despite (or because of) its sour attitude, it is a surprisingly skilled close-range fighter that uses acidic punches and body slams. It smells strongly of vinegar and regret.

### Evolution

Mickle evolves from Plantcas or Ickus after being cured of Poison status 3 times.

**Previous Evolution:** Plantcas or Ickus (poison status)
