# Rootick

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Angry Root Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #025  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 23.1 lbs (10.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Angry Root Mickémon. Always irritable. It trips people with vines and then acts innocent."

### Biology
Rootick is perpetually in a bad mood. Its vines are constantly twitching with irritation, and it has a sour face that rarely changes expression. It enjoys tripping Trainers and other Mickémon with its vines, then acting completely innocent afterward. Rootick emits a stronger, more pungent odor than Plantcas and seems to take genuine pleasure in making others uncomfortable.

### Evolution

Evolves from Plantcas at level 34.  Evolves into Druimick if it levels while knowing a Fairy-type move.

**Previous Evolution:** Plantcas (Personality Value split)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Druimick (Fairy-type Move)
