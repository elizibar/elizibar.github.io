# Planthat

**The Big Hat Mickémon**

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Big Hat Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 26.5 lbs (12.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Big Hat Mickémon. The hat is suspiciously large. Some researchers believe the hat is the true organism."

### Biology
Planthat is easily recognized by its absurdly oversized hat, which provides shade for itself and smaller Mickémon. Some researchers believe the hat itself may be the true organism, with the body underneath serving mostly as support. It is calmer and more easygoing than its counterpart Rootick, often using its massive hat as an umbrella or a parasol while lounging lazily in the sun.

### Evolution

Evolves from Plantcas at level 34.
