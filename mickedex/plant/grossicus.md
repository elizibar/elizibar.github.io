# Grossicus

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Compost Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 52.9 lbs (24.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Compost Mickémon. Mushrooms and weird growths cover its body. The stench is legendary."

### Biology
Grossicus is a walking compost heap of a Mickémon, covered in mushrooms, mold, rotting leaves, and suspicious slime. The stench it produces is legendary and powerful enough to make even Poison-types faint. Mushrooms and strange growths sprout all over its body, and it leaves a trail of fertilizer wherever it goes. Despite its repulsive appearance, Grossicus seems oddly proud of its "natural" state.

### Evolution

Grossicus evolves from Ickus at level 30.  When a Lead Stone is used on Grossicus, any empty spots on a Trainer's team are filled with Mickworms.

**Previous Evolution:** Ickus (Level 30)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickworms (when Leaf Stone is used)
