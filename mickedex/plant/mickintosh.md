# Mickintosh

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Apple Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** Weight: 26.5 lbs (12.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Apple Mickémon. It is famous for dropping shiny, techy seeds that can supposedly grow into powerful machines."

### Biology
Mickintosh is a round, shiny metallic apple with a prominent leaf on top that resembles a classic computer cursor. Its body has a sleek silver-and-green finish, complete with tiny glowing "byte" patterns across its skin. It has stubby arms and legs, and its mullet tuft pokes out from under a stylish metallic leaf-hat.

True to its name, Mickintosh is obsessed with "innovation." It constantly drops strange metallic seeds that look like USB drives or microchips. These seeds are said to grow into powerful (but often glitchy) tech-themed plants if planted. Mickintosh acts superior to other Grass-types and loves showing off its "revolutionary" features, even though most of them are just shiny marketing.

### Evolution

Mickintosh evolves from Plantcas or Ickus if a Sweet Apple, Syrupy Apple, or Tart Apple is used on it.
