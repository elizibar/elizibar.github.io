# Ickus

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Swamp Gremlin Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #026  
**Height:** 2'07" (0.8 m)  
**Weight:** 31.3 lbs (14.2 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Swamp Gremlin Mickémon. Coated in slime and moss. Being near it is a guaranteed way to get poisoned."

### Biology
Ickus is a slimy, moss-covered swamp gremlin that thrives in stagnant water and mud. Its body is coated in a layer of questionable goo that leaves a sticky trail behind it. Ickus enjoys giving opponents status conditions simply by existing near them. It has a mischievous personality and takes particular delight in making cleaner Mickémon uncomfortable with its smell and slime.

### Evolution

Ickus evolves from a Mickus with Low Friendship when a Leaf Stone is used on it.  It evolves into Grossicus at level 30.  If it levels while holding a berry, it will evolve into Mickberry.  If any Apple is used on it then it evolves into Mickintosh.  If cured during combat 3 times of the Pouson status, it evolves into Mickle.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Low Friendship + Leaf Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Grossicus (Level 30), Mickberry (Berry), Mickintosh (Any Apple), Mickle (Poison status), Druimick (Fairy-type move)
