# Slicus

**Type:** Grass / Dark  
**Classification:** Razor Assassin Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #028  
**Height:** 2'11" (0.9 m)  
**Weight:** 35.3 lbs (16.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Razor Assassin Mickémon. Its leaves are sharper than knives. Never trust its innocent smile."

### Biology
Slicus is a dangerous swamp assassin covered in razor-sharp leaves and vines. It has a deceptively innocent smile that hides its predatory nature. Slicus moves silently through murky waters and dense foliage, striking with surgical precision using its bladed leaves. It takes great pride in its cutting ability and is known to meticulously maintain its leaf blades. Despite its dangerous reputation, it still retains a hint of the classic Mickus derpiness.

### Evolution

Slicus evolves from an Ickus holding a Razor Claw when it levels at Night.

**Previous Evolution:** Ickus (Razor Claw + leveled up at night)
