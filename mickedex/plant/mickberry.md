# Mickberry

**Type:** Grass / Poison  
**Classification:** Berry Hoarder Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'03" (0.7 m)
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Berry Hoarder Mickémon. It is obsessed with collecting berries and glues them all over its body since it cannot grow any itself."

### Biology
Mickberry is a small, shrubby Mickus covered in twigs, leaves, and an ever-growing collection of berries glued haphazardly across its body. It has a permanent look of mild panic on its face as it constantly searches for more berries to add to its collection. Because it cannot grow berries of its own, it frantically scavenges them from the wild and glues them onto itself using a sticky sap it produces.

The more berries it collects, the happier (and heavier) it becomes. Different berries give it different colored patches and slight boosts in personality: a Cheri Berry might make it feisty, while a Sitrus Berry makes it oddly cheerful. Mickberry is known to get extremely distressed if any of its glued-on berries fall off.

### Evolution

Mickberry evolves from Plantcas or Ickus if it levels up while holding any Berry.

**Previous Evolution:** Plantcas or Ickus (Any Berry)
