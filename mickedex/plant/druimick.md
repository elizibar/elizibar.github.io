# Druimick

**Type:** Grass / Fairy  
**Classification:** Druid Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #032  
**Height:** 2'05" (0.75 m)  
W**eight:** 27.6 lbs (12.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Druid Mickémon. It practices ancient nature magic while wearing a comically large baseball cap as its hood."

### Biology
Druimick is a small, mystical Mickus that has taken up the ways of the druid. It wears flowing leafy robes and carries a gnarled wooden staff topped with a glowing berry. However, instead of the traditional druid hood, it wears an oversized, slightly tilted baseball cap with a the usual Mickus emblem on the front.

It speaks in a mysterious, wise tone and claims to commune with the spirits of the forest... though it mostly just talks to trees and then argues with them. Druimick is surprisingly skilled at nature magic, able to grow vines, summon healing pollen, and curse opponents with status conditions. It still retains the classic Mickus derpiness, it often trips over its own robes and loses its staff mid-spell.

### Evolution

Evolves from Planthat or Rootick when they learn a Fairy-type move.

**Previous Evolution:** Planthat or Rootick (Fairy-type move)
