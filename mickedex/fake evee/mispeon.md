# Mispeon

**Type:** Psychic  
**Classification:** Fake Espeon Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'07" (0.8 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Fake Espeon Mickémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as an Espeon."

### Biology
Mispeon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is an Espeon. It has dyed its body lavender, added a gem-like forehead decoration, and practices its "elegant psychic pose" constantly. Its mullet tuft has been styled into a graceful crest, and it still wears its signature hat (now modified with psychic crystal decorations). It tries to act mysterious and graceful like a real Espeon but frequently fails, by tripping over its own tail or accidentally using its psychic powers to levitate snacks instead of enemies. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Mickémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Espeon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when it levels at Day with High Friendship.
