# Mylveon

**Type:** Fairy  
**Classification:** Fake Sylveon Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 3’03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 51.8 lbs (23.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Fake Sylveon Mickémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Sylveon."

### Biology
Mylveon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Sylveon. It has dyed its body pink and white, attached flowing ribbon-like fabric strips to its body, and decorated itself with bows and sparkly accessories. Its mullet tuft has been styled into elegant, flowing "feelers," and it still wears its signature hat (now adorned with a big bow and ribbons). It tries to act gentle, loving, and graceful like a real Sylveon, often prancing around and trying to spread "healing ribbons" and affection, but frequently fails via tripping over its fake ribbons, getting tangled in them, or accidentally whacking others with the poorly attached bows. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Mickémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Sylveon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee while knowing a Fairy-type move and having High Friendship.
