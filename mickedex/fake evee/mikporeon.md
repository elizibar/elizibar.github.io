# Mikporeon

**The Fake Vaporeon Mickémon**

**Type:** Water  
**Classification:** Fake Vaporeon Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'11" (0.9 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)	  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Fake Vaporeon Mickémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Vaporeon."

### Biology
Mikporeon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Vaporeon. It has dyed its body blue, added fin-like attachments, and practices its "graceful swimming" pose constantly. Its mullet tuft has been styled into a fin-like crest, and it still wears its signature hat (now modified to look like a Vaporeon fin). It tries to act elegant and mysterious like a real Vaporeon but frequently fails, splashing around clumsily, getting its fake fins stuck, or accidentally saying "Mick!" instead of "Vaporeon!" It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Mickémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Vaporeon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when a Water Stone is used on it.
