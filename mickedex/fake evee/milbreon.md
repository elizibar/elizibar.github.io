# Milbreon

**The Fake Umbreon Mickémon**

**Type:** Dark  
**Classification:** Fake Umbreon Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Fake Umbreon Mickémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as an Umbreon."

### Biology
Milbreon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is an Umbreon. It has dyed its body black with glowing yellow rings, added sleek "fur" attachments, and practices its "mysterious night pose" constantly. Its mullet tuft has been styled into a sleek, dark crest, and it still wears its signature hat (now modified with moon decorations). It tries to act cool and mysterious like a real Umbreon but frequently fails, by tripping over its own rings or accidentally glowing too brightly when excited. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Mickémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Umbreon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when it levels at Night with High Friendship.
