# Milteon

**The Fake Jolteon Mickémon**

**Type:** Electric  
**Classification:** Fake Jolteon Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'07" (0.8 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Fake Jolteon Mickémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Jolteon."

### Biology
Milteon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Jolteon. It has dyed its body yellow, added spiky "fur" attachments, and practices its "electric spark" pose constantly. Its mullet tuft has been styled into sharp spikes, and it still wears its signature hat (now modified with lightning bolt decorations). It tries to act fast and energetic like a real Jolteon but frequently fails by tripping over its own spikes or accidentally zapping itself. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Mickémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Jolteon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when a Thunder Stone is used on it.
