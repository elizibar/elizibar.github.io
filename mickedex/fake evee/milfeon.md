# Milfeon

**The Fake Leafeon Mickémon**

**Type:** Grass  
**Classification:** Fake Leafeon Mickémon  
**Height:** 2’11" (0.9 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Fake Leafeon Pokémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Leafeon."

### Biology
Milfeon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Leafeon. It has dyed its body green, attached leaf-like decorations all over itself, and even glued real leaves and twigs to its fur for authenticity. Its mullet tuft has been styled into a leafy mane, and it still wears its signature hat (now camouflaged with foliage). It tries to act calm and nature-attuned like a real Leafeon, often meditating under trees or photosynthesizing in the sun, but frequently fails by sneezing from pollen, getting its fake leaves tangled, or absentmindedly eating the decorations it glued on. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Pokémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Leafeon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when a Leaf Stone is used on it.
