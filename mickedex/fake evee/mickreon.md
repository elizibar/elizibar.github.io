# Mickreon

**The Fake Flareon Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire  
**Classification:** Fake Flareon Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'11" (0.9 m)  
**Weight:** 55.1 lbs (25.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Fake Flareon Pokémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Flareon."

### Biology
Mickreon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Flareon. It has dyed its body orange-red, added fluffy "fur" attachments, and practices its "fiery mane" pose constantly. Its mullet tuft has been styled into a dramatic flaming crest, and it still wears its signature hat (now modified with flame decorations). It tries to act elegant and fiery like a real Flareon but frequently fails and accidentally sets its own hat on fire or trips over its fluffy tail. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Pokémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Flareon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when it has a Fire Stone used on it.
