# Mickeon

**The Fake Glaceon Mickémon**

**Type:** Ice  
**Classification:** Fake Glaceon Mickémon  
**Height:** 2’07" (0.8 m)  
**Weight:** 57.1 lbs (25.9 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Fake Glaceon Pokémon. It is a Mickus desperately trying to pass as a Glaceon."

### Biology
Mickeon is a Mickus that has fully committed to pretending it is a Glaceon. It has dyed its body light blue, added crystalline ice-like attachments, and covers itself in frost and snow whenever possible. Its mullet tuft has been styled into elegant, icy spikes, and it still wears its signature hat (now decorated with frost patterns and small icicles). It tries to act graceful and cool like a real Glaceon, often posing dramatically on frozen surfaces or creating “ice sculptures” (which are mostly just misshapen snowballs), but frequently fails by slipping on its own ice, melting in warm weather, or getting its fake crystals stuck to everything. It is very dedicated to the bit and gets genuinely upset when other Pokémon point out that it’s obviously not a real Glaceon.

### Evolution
Evolves from Meekee when an Ice Stone is used.
