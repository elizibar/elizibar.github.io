# Mickoggoth

**The Shoggoth Mickémon**

**Type:** Dark / Sound  
**Classification:** Shoggoth Mickémon  
**Height:** Varies (typically 6'07" / 2.0 m)  
**Weight:** Varies (typically 264.6 lbs / 120.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Shoggoth Mickémon. It is a shapeless horror that can assume any form, constantly muttering in unknown tongues."

### Biology
Mickoggoth is a nightmarish, shapeless mass of bubbling flesh, eyes, mouths, and tentacles. It constantly shifts and reforms, sometimes mimicking other Mickémon, sometimes forming grotesque parodies of Mickus, and sometimes becoming something completely unrecognizable. A tattered, slime-covered hat somehow remains on its "head" no matter how much it changes shape. It communicates in a cacophony of distorted voices and sounds, often muttering incomprehensible phrases that can drive listeners mad. Despite its eldritch nature, it still retains a hint of the classic Mickus derpiness and occasional forms a confused face or gets its own tentacles tangled.

It serves as a guardian and servant of deeper horrors in the Abyss.

### Evolution
Transformed from any Water-type (except Mickraken and Mickeel) by Team Deep Sea in the Abyss.
