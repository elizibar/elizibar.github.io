# Deepmick

**Type:** Water / Dark  
**Classification:** Deep One Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 44.1 lbs (20.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Deep One Mickémon. It is a pale, fish-like horror that serves the will of greater entities below."

### Biology
Deepmick is a pale, eyeless eel-like Mickus with too many teeth and glowing lures on its head. Its skin is slick and translucent, revealing dark shapes moving beneath. It has small, webbed hands and a tattered hat fused to its head. It is a common servant of the deeper horrors and is often seen in large groups, whispering in an unknown language. While it is horrifying to look at, it is surprisingly social with other Deepmicks and will sometimes try to "befriend" surface Mickémon by offering strange gifts from the abyss. It still retains a hint of the classic Mickus derpiness, getting its lures tangled or forgetting what it was doing mid-whisper.

### Evolution
Transformed from Mickeel by Team Deep Sea in the Abyss.
