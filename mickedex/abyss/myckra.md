### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Deep One Mickémon (Female). Myckra is a massive fish-woman horror said to be the mother of many Deep Ones."

### Biology
Myckra is a towering, fish-like horror with graceful yet terrifying features. Her body is covered in iridescent scales, and she has long, flowing tentacles that resemble hair. She wears ancient jewelry made from sunken treasure and carries an aura of maternal authority mixed with cosmic horror. She is said to be the mother of many Deep Ones and presides over spawning grounds in the Abyss. Like Migon, she retains a hint of Mickus derpiness and occasionally gets distracted by shiny objects or hums old sea shanties.

### Evolution
Transformed from Deepmick by Team Deep Sea in the Abyss.
