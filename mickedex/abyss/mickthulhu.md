# Mickthulhu

**Type:** Water / Dark  
**Classification:** Great Old One Mickémon  
**Height:** 12'06" (3.8 m)  
**Weight:** 771.6 lbs (350.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Great Old One Mickémon. It is a being of incomprehensible power and madness."

### Biology
Mickthulhu is a colossal, nightmarish fusion of Mickus and ancient sea horror. Its body is a writhing mass of tentacles, scales, and glowing runes, with a distorted Mickus face at the center. A tattered, barnacle-covered hat sits atop its head, and its mullet has become a crown of writhing tendrils. It constantly whispers incomprehensible secrets that can drive Trainers and Mickémon mad. Despite its godlike power and terrifying appearance, it still has a hint of the classic Mickus derpiness, occasionally getting its tentacles tangled or complaining about "these newfangled dimensions."

It was created by Team Deep Sea as the ultimate weapon, but it has its own incomprehensible agenda.

### Evolution
Transformed from Mickraken by Team Deep Sea in the Abyss.
