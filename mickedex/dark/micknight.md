# Micknight

**Type:** Dark / Ghost  
**Classification:** Edgelord Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 99.2 lbs (45.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Edgelord Mickémon. Writes bad poetry at 3 AM and thinks shadows make it mysterious."

### Biology
Micknight is an edgelord Mickus draped in black and purple fabric with glowing red eyes. It wears a dramatic cloak and tries extremely hard to look mysterious and intimidating. It writes terrible poetry at 3 AM and believes that standing dramatically in the shadows makes it deep and complex. In reality, it is still just as clumsy and derpy as other Mickus forms.

### Evolution

Micknight evolves from Mickus when a Dusk Stone is used on it.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Dusk Stone)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Micklord
