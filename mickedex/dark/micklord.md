# Micklord

**Type:** Dark / Ghost  
**Classification:** Shadow King Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #053  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 132.3 lbs (60.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Shadow King Mickémon. It sits on a tiny throne made of darkness and writes terrible poetry."

### Biology
Micklord is a dramatic edgelord Mickus that sits atop a tiny floating throne made of solidified shadows. It is draped in dark regal robes and has glowing red eyes that pierce through darkness. While it tries to project an aura of ultimate power and mystery, it still writes terrible poetry in its spare time and gets sulky when no one appreciates its "deep" lyrics. Its presence causes dramatic wind and dim lighting wherever it goes.

### Evolution

Micklord evolves from Micknight at level 40.

**Previous Evolution:** Micknight (Level 40)  
**Next Evolution(s):** -
