# Mickaboo

**Type:** Ghost / Fairy  
**Classification:** Peekaboo Scarer Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'00" (0.6 m)  
**Weight:** 13.2 lbs (6.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Peekaboo Scarer Mickémon. Mickaboo loves playing peekaboo, but its version is absolutely terrifying."

### Biology
Mickaboo is a small, deceptively cute Ghost/Fairy Mickus that wears a flowing white sheet with eye holes. It constantly plays "peekaboo" but its version involves suddenly appearing inches from someone’s face with glowing eyes and a distorted grin. What makes it especially scary is how genuinely cheerful it is while terrifying people. It finds the screams of surprise delightful and will giggle maniacally after a successful scare.

### Evolution

Evolves from Grim Mick when it levels up after learning a Fairy-type move.
