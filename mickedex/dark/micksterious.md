# Micksterious

**The Enigmatic Figure Mickémon**

**Type:** Ghost / Psychic  
**Classification:** Enigmatic Figure Mickémon  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 99.2 lbs (45.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Enigmatic Figure Mickémon. Micksterious is shrouded in mystery and is said to know secrets that no other Mickémon does."

### Biology
Micksterious is a tall, cloaked figure wrapped in swirling shadows and psychic energy. Its face is hidden behind a veil of darkness, with only two glowing eyes visible. It speaks in riddles and seems to know things it shouldn’t. It enjoys appearing suddenly in front of Trainers to deliver cryptic messages before vanishing again. While it tries to maintain an aura of ultimate mystery, it still has moments of pure Mickus derpiness when its cloak gets caught on something.

It is known to say things such as "You must master your rage, or your rage will become your master."

### Evolution

Evolves from Micklord or Grim Mick.
