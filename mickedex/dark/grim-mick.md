# Grim Mick

**Type:** Ghost / Dark  
**Classification:** Reaper Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #054  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 110.2 lbs (50.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Reaper Mickémon. Carries a scythe tail. Still writes terrible poetry between soul collections."

### Biology
Grim Mick is a somber, reaper-like Mickus draped in a tattered black cloak with a large scythe-like tail. It carries itself with dramatic melancholy and still finds time to write terrible emo poetry between soul-collecting duties. Its presence causes dramatic fog and chilling winds. Despite its intimidating appearance and grim profession, it remains fundamentally a Mickus at heart: clumsy, a bit dramatic, and secretly soft.

### Evolution

Grim Mick evolves from Micknight when a Reaper Cloth is used on it.

**Previous Evolution:** Micknight (Reaper Cloth)  
**Next Evolution(s):** -
