# Mickster

**Type:** Dark / Fairy  
**Classification:** Illusionist Trickster Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 88.2 lbs (40.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Illusionist Trickster Mickémon. Mickster loves playing elaborate pranks using illusions and dark fairy magic."

### Biology
Mickster is a flashy, theatrical trickster Mickus that wears a sleek black-and-pink stage magician outfit with a flowing cape. It carries a wand made of twisted dark energy and is a master of illusions. It loves pulling elaborate pranks on both allies and opponents, making them see things that aren’t there, swapping their items, or making them suddenly dance uncontrollably. Despite its mischievous nature, it has a charming personality and always takes a bow after a successful trick.
### Evolution
