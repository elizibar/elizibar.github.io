# Mickshake

**Type:** Fairy / Ice  
**Classification:** Milkshake Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Milkshake Mickémon. It is a delicious-looking milkshake with a straw and whipped cream on top. It is very sweet and popular with children."

### Biology
Mickshake is a living milkshake in a tall glass with a swirl of whipped cream on top, a cherry, and a bendy straw. Its "body" is a thick, creamy liquid with colorful sprinkles floating inside. It has big, happy eyes and a cheerful personality. It is incredibly sweet and popular with children, who love to drink its healing cream (which somehow replenishes itself). It can fire blasts of creamy milkshake or freeze opponents with its icy-cold temperature. Like Mickreme, it is sensitive to heat and can start melting if it gets too excited.
