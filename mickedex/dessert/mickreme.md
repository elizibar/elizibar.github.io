# Mickreme

**Type:** Fairy  
**Classification:** Dessert Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 24.3 lbs (11.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Dessert Mickémon. A swirly ice cream idol. Melts under pressure but tastes delicious."

### Biology
Mickreme is a colorful, swirly soft-serve ice cream Mickémon with a cherry on top of its head and sprinkles across its body. It has a perpetually cheerful expression and a sweet scent that attracts other Mickémon. However, it is quite sensitive to heat and will start melting if it gets too excited or stays in direct sunlight for too long. Mickreme is beloved by children but frustrating for Trainers who have to clean up the resulting mess after battles.

### Evolution

Mickreme evolves from Mickus if the Trainer spins the stick during a level up while Mickus is holding a Sweet Berry.
