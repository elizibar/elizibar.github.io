# Mikehammer

**Type:** Fighting/Steel  
**Classification:** Construction Crusher Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #013  
**Height:** 5'11" (1.8 m)  
**Weight:** 682.3 lbs (309.5 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Construction Crusher Mickémon. It smashes through boulders and buildings with equal enthusiasm."

### Biology
Mikehammer is an absolute monster of a bovine Mickus. Its body is covered in thick, riveted steel plating, and it carries a massive demolition hammer that it swings with terrifying power. A reinforced hard hat sits atop its head, now fused with heavy metal armor. While it retains Mike’s friendly personality and still calls people "buddy," its sheer destructive capability is on another level. It loves construction sites, not for building, but for enthusiastically "renovating" anything in its path.

Mikehammer is happiest when it gets to smash large objects into smaller ones. It has been known to accidentally level abandoned buildings while trying to help with demolition work.



### Evolution

Mikehammer evolves from Mike or Mikross if they level while holding a Metal Coat.  Mikehammer evolves into Mikechamp at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mike or Mikross (Metal Coat)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mikechamp (Level 38)
