# Mikechamp

**The Gym Leader Wannabe Mickémon**

**Type:** Fighting  
**Classification:** Gym Leader Wannabe Mickémon  
Height: 6'03" (1.9 m)  
Weight: 815.7 lbs (370.0 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Gym Leader Wannabe Mickémon. It dreams of one day running its own Gym and spends most of its time practicing dramatic poses and entrance speeches."

### Biology
Mikechamp is the ultimate evolution of the Mike line, a massive, heavily muscled bovine Mickus that has fully committed to becoming a Gym Leader. It wears a championship belt, a referee-style shirt stretched to its limits, and a headband that says "#1 Dad."

It constantly practices its would-be Gym Leader speech, poses dramatically with its massive fists, and challenges random Trainers to battles while yelling "Welcome to the Buddy Badge Gym!" Even when it loses, it gives an enthusiastic thumbs-up and says "Good effort, sport!" Mikechamp is incredibly strong but its battles often devolve into motivational speeches and dad jokes mid-fight.

### Evolution

Mikechamp evolves from Mikehammer at level 38.
