# Mike Y

**Type:** Ground / Fighting  
**Classification:** Construction Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #011  
**Height:** 5'03" (1.6 m)  
**Weight:** 352.7 lbs (160.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Construction Mickémon. Wears a hard hat and carries a comically large wrench. It builds things just so it can knock them down again."

### Biology
Mike Y is an absolute unit of a bovine Mickus, now even larger and more muscular than Mike. It wears a bright yellow hard hat and carries a comically oversized wrench. Mike Y loves construction and demolition in equal measure. It will happily build a bridge one moment and knock it down the next. Despite its immense strength and imposing size, it remains incredibly friendly and still calls everyone "buddy."

### Evolution
Mike Y evolves from Mike at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mike (Level 38)
