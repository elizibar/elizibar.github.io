# Mike

**The Trucker Dad Mickémon**

**Type:** Normal  
**Classification:** Trucker Dad Mickémon  
**Height:** 4'07" (1.4 m)  
**Weight:** 198.4 lbs (90.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Trucker Dad Mickémon. Produces delicious milk and tells awful dad jokes. It calls everyone 'sport' and gives headpats after battles."

### Biology
Mike is a large, friendly bovine Mickus wearing a trucker hat. It has a muscular yet approachable build, kind eyes, and a permanent gentle smile. Mike produces high-quality milk that can energize allies and is famous for its terrible dad jokes. It treats its Trainer like its own kid, frequently calling them "sport" and offering encouraging headpats (which can sometimes knock the recipient over due to Mike’s size).

### Evolution

Mike evolves from Mickus when teamed with Miltank or if a a Milk BOttle is used.  Mike evolves into Mike Y at level 38.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Party with Miltank or use Milk Bottle)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mike Y (Level 38)
