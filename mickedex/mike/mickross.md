# Mickross

**Type:** Normal / Ground  
**Classification:** Armored Tank Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #012  
**Height:** 5'05" (1.65 m)  
**Weight:** 485.0 lbs (220.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Armored Tank Mickémon. A heavily fortified bovine Mickus. Its defense is so high that attacks just bounce off its hard hat."

### Biology
Mickross is a heavily armored, tank-like bovine Mickus. Its body is covered in thick metal plating and reinforced with a massive hard hat that doubles as a shield. Despite its intimidating, fortress-like appearance, it retains the friendly and helpful personality of Mike. It enjoys giving gentle (yet still very heavy) headbutts and is surprisingly good at protecting its teammates in battle.

### Evolution

Mickross evolves from Mickus when a Protector is used on it.  Evolves into Mikehammer if it levels while holding a Metal Coat.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Protector)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mikehammer (Metal Coat)
