# Team Deep Space

**Warp Seekers**

Tema Deep Space seeks to find The Warp. It isn't so much a location, as a pulse of energy picked up by one of the team's Space Rangers. The Warp is reputed to be both a gateway to another world, a lost place called Mickosa ruled by an alien being named the Mickinyellow. Team Deep Space wants to use the power of Mickosa to help guide humanity to a future among the stars, and also prove itself superior to their rivals in Team Deep Sea.

## Space Rangers

Team Deep Space is led by their Space Rangers, the founding members who have led them from the beginning.

### Inkus

Quit possibly the only member of Team Deep Space whose identity is known, Inkus is better known as the "Mad Cow Scientist." He maintains a laboratory in an elaborate lighter-than-air classic-styled airship, crewed with bovine Mickémon and filled with an extensive array of high-tech components which bely the outdated style of the craft itself.

### Morgius

Not much is known about the Space Ranger code-named Morgius other than her position as the leader of a sub-group called "Pidges" which are the most active members of Team Deep Space. The Pidges can be seen, wearing their Pidge-masks, as they carry out missions across Cordia for her.

### Impus

The identity of this Space Ranger is unknown, as are their whereabouts.

## The Warp

The Warp is believed to be a concentrated energy source that fell to Earth from space, possibly causing a massive volanic eruption which formed the Cordia caldera. The concentration of energy from the Warp is high enough that Inkus was able to measure its presence all the way from his University posting in the Galar region. It was this measurement that led to Inkus raising funds and forming the team to search for it.

But the Warp isn't solely an energy source, it is believed to possess enough poweer that it can distort space and open pathways to other worlds. Communications received by Team Deep Space purport that it will lead them to a land known as Mickosa, where a powerful ally awaits to help them bring humanity to the stars.

Oddly there are ancient stories about Mickosa. It supposed has a binary star system, and its great city sits on the edge of a lake. The sky itself is bright, and the stars are dim pin-pricks in the heavens. Its towers rise so high the moon eclipses them, instead of them blocking it.

## Associated Locations

### The Sco'ish Cow

The air-borne laboratory of Professor Inkus, the so-called "Mad Cow Scientist" who travels acrosss Cordia. The on-board laboratory is filled with the finest laboratory equipment money can buy, from Team Deep Space's mysterious backers.

Much of the ship is built around measuring and locating the Warp, but the signal is too strong and the instruments are incapable of providing accurate readings. A smaller section of the lab is setup for the study of rare creatures, soil samples, and even paleontology.

### The Bakery

While there is no official proof that the Bakery is connected to Team Deep Space, many Pidges have been seen in the environs around it.  Of course, that could just be coincidence because of the proximity of Mount Lucas just up-stream from that location.

## Goals

Team Deep Space's primary mission is to reach the stars, sending humanity out to discover strange new worlds. To get to the stars they need a powersource capable of sending a space craft to other worlds, and they hope to find such a power at The Warp. Only this close to it, the readings of the Warp's energy signature are overpowering. There are stories of an artifact called the Crystal Skull that can lead to the Warp, and so Team Deep Space is trying to find it first.

* Find the lost Crystal Skull
* Harness the power of The Warp
* Lead humanity to the stars

## Modus Operandi

Team Deep Space operates primarily in secrecy, with only their masked Pidge operatives being obviously part of the group. The scientist, Inkus, is the only known member of the team, and that's only because he openly promoted the cause, raised money, and began his search to public fanfare. Years of thwarted research have led to him being labeled something of a crank, which only led to the rest of the team going deep undercover.

Pidges are armed with a unique mix of Rock- and Flying-type creatures, and also ones that are obviously of origin from outerspace. Across Cordia they swoop in with the element of surprise, grab the information or artifact that they've come for, and then vanish into the sky from which they came.
