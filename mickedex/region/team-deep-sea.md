# Team Deep Sea

**Abyss Seekers**  

Team Deep Sea seeks the Abyss. It is the fabled location of the sunken city of M'lyeh, an ancient place of power built by Mickémon. M'lyeh is reputed to hold their strange and mysterious power, and it is this power that Team Deep Sea wishes to harness in their long-running feud with Team Deep Space by proving that the bounty of the sea is where humanity's future lies.

## Leviathans

Team Deep Sea is led by a council of Leviathans. Their exact identities are unknown to the public, and even their exact number is treated as a secret.

### Sticcus

One of the Leviathans of Team Deep Sea. It's not entirely clear, but they say that Sticcus is the same trainer that operates in the Cove, teaching younger trainers how best to handle their Water-types.

Her reputation is one of being a stern task master that doesn't stand for anyone's shenanigans.

### Dee-Emm

Dee-Emm is another Leviathan of Team Deep Sea. Rumors disagree on Dee-Emm's gender, but they all agree that Dee-Emm possesses red skin.

### Eggus

This mysterious individual is known to be the primary source of funding for Team Deep Sea, but his identity and whereabouts are unknown.  It is also unknown if Eggus actually holds the rank of Leviathan on the team, but the name is frequently associated with the team's activities.

## The Abyss

The physical location of The Abyss is unknown, except that ancient legends from Micklantis and Lemickia place it somewhere near the Cordia region, sunken deep under the ocean.  egends tell that the ancient gods rose up against the inhabitend of M'lyeh and destroyed its otherworldly inhabitants, condemning them to an existence in the darkest, coldest part of the sea, a great sunken world of pitch black, where the water is below freezing and yet because of intense pressure it refuses to freeze into ice

M'lyeh is described by the Micklanteans are being a "nightmare corpse-city", ancient and alien, with Cyclopean stylings and strange unearthly masonry carved into hideous bas reliefs. The enemies of Micklantis, the ancient naval power of Lemickia, describe the city's geometry as being slimy and ever-shifting as if space itself were somehow squirming at the horror occupying it.

The city is said to predate humanity's existence, and even that of most other modern life. If that were true, and its colossal monoliths, alien green stone, and strange geometries were so ancient it begs the question of how the stories of M'lyeh survived and were passed down to the present day.

### M'lyeh

This text was the only readable portion of a journal which was recovered with a trainer that had returned from a journey into the Jungles of Discootia:

>There is a city in the deep where shadows play,
>Where sunken towers twist in endless night,
>And pale things drift through waters black as death,
>Beneath the weight of oceans without light.
>The streets are lined with stone no mortal made,
>And doors stand open to the crushing sea,
>Where once the faithful sang their monstrous hymns
>To gods that sleep in vaults no light can see.
>
>I dream of M'lyeh when the surface fades,
>And hear the bubbles rising through the gloom,
>The muffled chants that echo through the dark,
>And know that I shall find my final tomb.
>For in those depths the old ones never died,
>They only wait beneath the crushing tide.

## Associated Locations

### The Cove

While the purported location of the Cove is known only to members of Team Deep Sea, it is believed to be on the inner-sea side of Lucasia, and is expected to be their headquarters. At least one Leviathan is supposed to reside there year-round, training Deep Sea operatives in how best to make use of their Water-types and coordinate the Team's activities in the region.

### The Grill

While there is no conclusive evidence that the Grill works with Team Deep Sea, more Deep Sea raids on other trainers, explorers, archeological or paleontological dig sites seem to happen near the Grill than in other areas of Cordia. That could just be a coincidence due to its location in the more habital part of Discootia and all the activity that entails.

## Goals

Team Deep Sea wants to help humanity make use of the vast deep ocean, harvesting resources, even making homes in the sunless sea. To be able to live in the deep ocean they need the ancient wisdom of the sunken city in the Abyss. To find where the Abyss is they need to decipher clues, unearth secrets, and perhaps even find a fossilized ancient inhabitant of the Abyss and bring it back to life so they can follow it back to its forgotten home.

* Find the prehistoric Mickus fossil
* Harness the resources of The Abyss
* Lead humanity to the Deeps

## Modus Operandi

Team Deep Sea is as mysterious and mercurial as the ocean. Their operatives have no distinct uniform, and yet they all obviously share styling and themes that help them identify each other. From the secret location known as the Cove, they go out across the region, across the sea, up rivers, far down into the deep, looking for the clues needed to find The Abyss.

Deep Sea members typically use a combination of Water-types and Dark-types in their operations. They will often emerge from the sea or a river, storm a trainer or explorer's camp, and then sink back into the deep before any organized resistance can be offered.
