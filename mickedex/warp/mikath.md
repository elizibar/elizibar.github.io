# Mikath

**Type:** Ground / Fire  
**Classification:** Planetoid Mickémon  
**Mickédex Number:** #???  
**Height:** 9'10" (3.0 m) (varies)  
**Weight:** 2,204.6 lbs (1,000.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Planetoid Mickémon. It is a living celestial body that devours everything in its path."

### Biology
Mikath is a massive, planetoid-shaped Mickus with a cracked, molten surface and glowing red eyes. Its body is a living world with continents of rock, oceans of magma, and a thin atmosphere of smoke. A tiny, tattered hat floats above its "north pole." It drifts through space consuming everything in its path, growing larger as it devours asteroids, ships, and unfortunate Mickémon. It has a slow, deliberate personality and speaks in deep, rumbling tones that shake the ground. Despite its apocalyptic nature, it still has a hint of Mickus derpiness, occasionally getting distracted by shiny satellites or complaining about "these newfangled planets."

### Evolution
Transforms when any Ground-type Mickus is exposed The Warp.
