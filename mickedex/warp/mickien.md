# Mickien

**The Grey Alien Mickémon**

**Type:** Psychic / Steel  
**Classification:** Grey Alien Mickémon  
**Height:** 3'03" (1.0 m)  
**Weight:** 66.1 lbs (30.0 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Grey Alien Pokémon. Mickien is a classic grey alien Mickus with a large head, black eyes, and a permanent curious expression. It still wears its signature hat."

### Biology
Mickien is the quintessential grey alien Mickus, smooth grey skin, oversized head, large black eyes, and a tiny body. It has long, slender fingers and still wears its signature hat (now slightly oversized on its big head). It floats slightly off the ground and communicates through telepathy, but it often just says "mick" in a distorted voice. It is fascinated by Earth culture and loves "probing" (mostly just poking and examining) other Mickémon and Trainers. Despite its advanced technology and mysterious aura, it is still very much a Mickus and will get distracted by shiny objects, trip over its own feet, and occasionally forget what it was doing mid-abduction.

### Evolution
Transformed when any Psychic-type Mickus is taken to The Warp.
