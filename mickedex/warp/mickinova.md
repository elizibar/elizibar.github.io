# Mickinova

**The Supernova Mickémon**

**Type:** Fire / Psychic  
**Classification:** Supernova Mickémon  
**Height:** 9'10" (3.0 m)  
**Weight:** 771.6 lbs (350.0 kg)  

### Short Mickédex Entry
"The Supernova Mickémon. It is a living star that is constantly exploding and reforming in cycles of destruction and rebirth."

### Biology
Mickinova is a massive, unstable star-like Mickus with a glowing core and layers of plasma and fire constantly shedding from its body. Its "face" is a bright, fiery Mickus expression with a tiny, scorched hat floating above it. It is in a perpetual state of supernova, exploding in brilliant bursts of fire and psychic energy, then slowly reforming only to explode again. Each cycle makes it slightly larger and brighter. It is incredibly powerful but unstable, and its mere presence can cause massive temperature spikes and psychic disturbances. Despite its apocalyptic nature, it still has the classic Mickus derpiness, occasionally getting distracted mid-explosion or complaining about "these newfangled cosmic cycles."

### Evolution
Transforms when a Fire-type Mickus is taken to The Warp.
