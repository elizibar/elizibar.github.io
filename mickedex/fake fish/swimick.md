# Swimick

**Delusional Fish Mickémon**

**Type:** Water  
**Classification:** Delusional Fish Mickémon  
**Height:** 1'08" (0.5 m)  
**Weight:** 14.3 lbs (6.5 kg)  

### Short Pokédex Entry
"The Delusional Fish Mickémon. Swimick is convinced it is a fish despite being a Mickus wearing swim fins and a snorkel."

### Biology
Swimick is a Mickus that has fully committed to the bit. It wears bright blue swim fins on both its hands and feet, a snorkel mask with a comically large tube, and refuses to acknowledge that it is not actually a fish. It "swims" by flopping around in water with great enthusiasm, occasionally trying (and failing) to breathe underwater. Despite its complete lack of gills or proper swimming ability, Swimick is incredibly proud of its "aquatic nature" and gets offended when reminded it is still a land-dwelling gremlin. It spends most of its time doing dramatic dolphin kicks and pretending it’s perfectly at home in the ocean, even in shallow puddles. Other Mickémon have learned not to question its life choices.

### Evolution
Swimick evolves from Mickus after traveling 1000 steps in water without touching land.  It evolves into Mickyfin.

**Previous Evolution:** Mickus (Travel in Water)  
**Next Evolution(s):** Mickyfin
