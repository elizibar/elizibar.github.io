# Mickyfin

**Delusional Poison Fish Mickémon**

**Type:** Water / Poison  
**Classification:** Delusional Poison Fish Mickémon  
**Height:** 2'04" (0.7 m)  
**Weight:** 38.6 lbs (17.5 kg)  

### Short Miickédex Entry
"The Delusional Poison Fish Mickémon. Mickyfin is absolutely convinced it is a majestic aquatic creature. It is not. The toxic purple sludge it constantly leaks makes swimming near it highly inadvisable."

### Biology
Mickyfin is what happens when Swimick fully doubles down on its fish delusion. It now wears even larger swim fins, a snorkel with two tubes, and a comically oversized pair of goggles. Its body is covered in a sickly purple toxic slime that it proudly claims is "just my natural scales." Despite being a Water/Poison type, Mickyfin refuses to acknowledge it is poisoning the water around it. It flops around with great enthusiasm, occasionally trying to do underwater spins and barrel rolls, only to belly-flop and release a cloud of toxic sludge. It still thinks it’s a graceful fish and gets genuinely offended when other Mickémon avoid swimming near it.Mickyfin’s favorite activity is "hunting" by charging straight at other Mickémon while yelling "I’m a predator!" before immediately getting distracted by a shiny pebble on the bottom.
